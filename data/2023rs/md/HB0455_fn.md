Video Lottery Terminal Proceeds - Purse Dedication Account - Ocean Downs 
Race Course Operating Loss Assistance 
 
   
This bill alters and makes permanent the annual distribution to Ocean Downs Race Course 
for operating loss assistance from the Purse Dedication Account (PDA). Specifically, the 
bill authorizes an annual distribution equal to up to $30,000 per live racing day, beginning 
in calendar 2023. The bill also repeals the calendar 2024 sunset date of the annual 
distribution to Ocean Downs Race Course from PDA. Under current law, Ocean Downs 
Race Course receives an annual distribution from PDA of up to $1.2 million through 
calendar 2024. The bill takes effect July 1, 2023. 
   
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  None, as the bill does not affect overall revenue for PDA. Total special fund 
expenditures from PDA do not change; however, the bill alters and redirects PDA revenues 
for distribution to Ocean Downs Race Course beginning in calendar 2023 as discussed 
below.     
  
Local Effect:  None.     
  
Small Business Effect:  Minimal.      
  
 
Analysis

$100.0 million, is distributed to PDA, which is under the authority of the Maryland Racing
