Frederick County - Alcoholic Beverages - Sunday Sales 
 
 
This bill extends the Sunday hours of sale for specified alcoholic beverages licenses in 
Frederick County. For most of the licenses specified in the bill, the modified Sunday hours 
of sale mirror the existing hours of sale for the other six days of the week. The bill takes 
effect July 1, 2023. 
 
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  None. 
  
Local Effect:  The bill does not materially affect Frederick County finances or operations. 
 
Small Business Effect:  Potentially meaningful. The bill’s extension of operating hours on 
Sundays for specified license holders could result in them realizing additional sales. 
 
 
Analysis 
 
Bill Summary/Current Law:  The bill extends the Sunday hours of sale for the following 
alcoholic beverage licenses in Frederick County:   
 
 
Class A beer, beer and wine (BW), and beer, wine, and liquor (BWL) licenses; 
 
Class B beer, BW, and BWL licenses; 
 
Class C beer, BW, and BWL licenses; 
 
Class B-CC (conference center) and Class B-DT (dinner theater) BWL licenses; 
 
Class EC (entertainment center) licenses; 
 
Class M-G (municipal golf course) BWL licenses; and 
 
hotel lobby licenses.

A summary of the bill’s changes to the Sunday hours of sale for each type of alcoholic 
beverages license is provided below. 
 
Class A Beer, BW, and BWL Licenses 
 
Class A licensees, subject to the restrictions of the specific license they hold, may sell 
alcoholic beverages at retail at the place described in their licenses. 
 
Current Hours:  6:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day, Monday through Saturday; on 
Sunday, sales permitted for license holders who have a special Sunday opening permit from 
10:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day. 
 
Proposed Hours:  6:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day, Monday through Saturday; on 
Sunday, sales permitted for license holders who have a special Sunday opening permit from 
6:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day. 
 
Class B Beer, and BW Licenses 
 
A Class B beer or BW licensee, subject to the restrictions of the specific license they hold, 
may sell alcoholic beverages at retail at a hotel or restaurant at the place described in the 
license (for on- and off-premises consumption). 
 
Current Hours:  6:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day, Monday through Saturday; on 
Sunday, sales permitted for on-premises consumption from 10:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the 
following day or for a specific event the board has approved, subject to hours set by the 
board; for off-premises consumption on Sunday, sales permitted from 1:00 p.m. to 
2:00 a.m. the following day. 
 
Proposed Hours:  6:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day, Monday through Sunday. 
 
Class B BWL Licenses 
 
There are two different types of Class B BWL licenses:  (1) a hotel or motel license and 
(2) a hotel or restaurant license. A Class B BWL (hotel or motel) license authorizes the sale 
of beer, wine, and liquor by the individual drink at any place on the hotel or motel premises; 
it also authorizes the sale of beer, wine, and liquor by the bottle for room service or 
specified on-premises events. A Class B BWL (hotel or restaurant) license authorizes the 
sale of beer, wine, and liquor for on-premises consumption where meals are prepared and 
served.

Class C Beer, BW, and BWL Licenses 
 
A Class C beer or BW licensee, subject to the restrictions of the specific license they hold, 
may sell alcoholic beverages at retail to members of a club and their guests at the place 
described in the license for on-premises consumption. A Class C BWL licensee, for 
on-premises consumption, is authorized to sell beer, wine, and liquor at a club at the place 
described in the license. For off-premises consumption, a Class C BWL licensee is 
authorized to sell collectible bottles of wine or liquor 30 calendar days before a special 
anniversary or special event. 
 
Current Hours:  6:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day, Monday through Saturday; on 
Sunday, 10:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day or for a specific event the board has 
approved, subject to hours set by the board. 
 
Proposed Hours:  6:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day, Monday through Sunday. 
 
Class B-CC (Conference Center) BWL Licenses 
 
A Class B-CC (conference center) BWL licensee is authorized to sell beer, wine, and liquor 
for consumption throughout the licensed premises, both indoors and outdoors, including 
meeting and banquet rooms, patios, verandas, and green spaces. 
 
Current Hours:  6:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day, Monday through Saturday; on 
Sunday, 11:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day. 
 
Proposed Hours:  6:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day, Monday through Sunday. 
 
Class B-DT (Dinner Theater) BWL Licenses 
 
A Class B-DT (dinner theater) BWL licensee is authorized to sell beer and wine, by the 
drink or bottle, and liquor by the drink for on-premises consumption. A licensee may not

Class EC (Entertainment Center) Licenses 
 
The board may issue a Class EC (entertainment center) license to a person for use in 
conjunction with (1) a Class 7 micro-brewery license that the person obtains from the 
Comptroller or (2) a Class B beer, wine, and liquor license that the person has been issued

Sunday, sales permitted from 6:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day or for a specific 
event the board has approved, subject to hours set by the board (hours are set in accordance 
with the hours prescribed for a Class B BWL license under § 20-2005 of the Alcoholic 
Beverages Article). 
 
Class M-G (Municipal Golf Course) BWL Licenses 
 
A Class M-G (municipal golf course) BWL licensee is authorized to sell beer, wine, and 
liquor for on-premises consumption on the land and in the facilities used for golfing 
purposes. 
 
Current Hours:  6:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m., Monday through Saturday; on Sunday, 
10:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. 
 
Proposed Hours:  6:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day, Monday through Saturday; on 
Sunday, sales permitted from 6:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day or for a specific 
event the board has approved, subject to hours set by the board (hours are set in accordance 
with the hours prescribed for a Class B BWL license under § 20-2005 of the Alcoholic 
Beverages Article).

Hotel Lobby Licenses 
 
A hotel lobby licensee is authorized to sell beer and wine by the bottle from a store in the

Current Hours:  6:00 a.m. to 2:00 a.m. the following day, Monday through Saturday; on
