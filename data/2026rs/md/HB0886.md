A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Certified Peer Recovery Specialists – Coverage Under the Maryland Medical
3 Assistance Program and Health Insurance and Workgroup on Certification

4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Medical Assistance Program and certain
5 insurers, nonprofit health service plans, health maintenance organizations, and
6 managed care organizations to provide coverage for services provided in any setting
7 by certain certified peer recovery specialists; requiring the Maryland Department of
8 Health to convene a certain workgroup on the certification process of peer recovery
9 specialists; and generally relating to certified peer recovery specialists.

5 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That the Maryland Department of

7 (1) convene a workgroup with the Maryland Addiction &
8 Behavioral–Health Professionals Certification Board and interested stakeholders to
9 identify ways to make the process of certifying peer recovery specialists more efficient,
10 including through reciprocity; and

11 (2) report to the Senate Finance Committee and the House Health
