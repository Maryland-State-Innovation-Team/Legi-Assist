A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Maryland Co–Location Energy Innovation and Reliability Act

3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Public Service Commission to adopt regulations related
4 to the construction of a generating station that is co–located with a data center but
5 is not interconnected with the electric transmission system or electric distribution
6 system; and generally relating to the co–location of data centers with generating
7 stations.

3 (B) THIS SECTION APPLIES ONLY TO A GENERATING STATION THAT:

4 (1) INCLUDES THE CO–LOCATION OF A DATA CENTER WITH THE

6 (2) DOES NOT INTERCONNECT WITH THE ELECTRIC TRANSMISSION
7 SYSTEM OR ELECTRIC DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM.

8 (C) THE COMMISSION SHALL ADOPT REGULATIONS TO IMPLEMENT
9 SPECIFIC POLICIES RELATED TO THE CONSTRUCTION OF A GENERATING STATION
10 UNDER THIS SECTION.

11 (D) THE REGULATIONS ADOPTED UNDER SUBSECTION (C) OF THIS SECTION
12 SHALL:

13 (1) DEFINE A GENERATING STATION AS AN INDEPENDENT RESOURCE
14 NOT SUBJECT TO STATE LAWS AND REGULATIONS RELATED TO RETAIL ELECTRIC
15 CUSTOMERS OR ELECTRICITY SUPPLIERS;

16 (2) CLARIFY THAT STATE–MANDATED ELECTRIC DISTRIBUTION
17 SYSTEM FEES OR RENEWABLE ENERGY PORTFOLIO STANDARD OBLIGATIONS DO
18 NOT APPLY TO THE GENERATING STATION OR ENERGY GENERATED AT THE
19 GENERATING STATION;

20 (3) ENSURE THAT A GENERATING STATION INCLUDES BACKUP
21 SOURCES OF ELECTRICITY FOR RELIABILITY, INCLUDING ON–SITE GENERATING
22 CAPACITY;

23 (4) DEVELOP ROBUST PROTECTIVE MEASURES TO ENSURE THAT A
24 GENERATING STATION DOES NOT INTERACT WITH THE ELECTRIC TRANSMISSION
25 SYSTEM OR ELECTRIC DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM;

26 (5) REQUIRE A GENERATING STATION TO INCLUDE CYBERSECURITY
27 SAFEGUARDS;

28 (6) ENSURE THAT AN APPLICANT FOR A CERTIFICATE OF PUBLIC
29 CONVENIENCE AND NECESSITY FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF A GENERATING
30 STATION DEMONSTRATE DURING THE APPLICATION PROCESS THAT THE
31 GENERATING STATION SHALL:
