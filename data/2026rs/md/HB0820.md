A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Handgun Permits – Special Endorsement for Security Clearance Holders

3 FOR the purpose of requiring that a handgun permit issued by the Secretary of State Police
4 bear a certain endorsement showing that the permit holder has enhanced privileges
5 to wear, carry, or transport a handgun under certain circumstances; providing that
6 a person who has a handgun permit issued under this Act may wear, carry, or
7 transport a handgun at any place where a certain law enforcement official is
8 authorized to wear, carry, or transport a handgun; and generally relating to handgun
9 permits.

1 BACKGROUND INVESTIGATION, THE SECRETARY DETERMINES THAT THE PERMIT
2 HOLDER IS:

3 (1) A CURRENT EMPLOYEE OF THE UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT

6 (2) A RETIRED EMPLOYEE OF THE UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT
7 WHO HELD A TOP SECRET/SENSITIVE COMPARTMENTED INFORMATION SECURITY
8 CLEARANCE FOR AT LEAST 10 CONSECUTIVE YEARS IMMEDIATELY PRECEDING
9 RETIREMENT FROM FEDERAL EMPLOYMENT.

10 (C) NOTWITHSTANDING ANY LAW OR ORDINANCE THAT PROHIBITS THE
11 WEARING, CARRYING, OR TRANSPORTING OF A HANDGUN AT A PLACE, A PERSON
12 WHO HAS A PERMIT BEARING AN ENDORSEMENT UNDER THIS SECTION MAY WEAR,
13 CARRY, OR TRANSPORT A HANDGUN AT ANY PLACE WHERE A LAW ENFORCEMENT
14 OFFICIAL IS AUTHORIZED TO WEAR, CARRY, OR TRANSPORT A HANDGUN IN THE
15 PERFORMANCE OF THE OFFICIAL’S DUTIES.

16 (D) THE SECRETARY SHALL ADOPT REGULATIONS NECESSARY TO CARRY
17 OUT THIS SECTION.

18 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
