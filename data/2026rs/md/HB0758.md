1 AN ACT concerning

2 Problem Gambling – Authorized Uses of Funds – Alterations

3 FOR the purpose of altering the purposes for which the Secretary of Health may make
4 grants from or agreements for the use of State and federal funds with respect to the

6 Maryland Department of Health may utilize the Problem Gambling Fund; and

1 (2) The Problem Gambling Fund is a special, nonlapsing fund that is not

3 (3) Money in the Problem Gambling Fund shall be invested and reinvested

5 (4) Except as provided in paragraph (5) of this subsection, expenditures
6 from the Problem Gambling Fund shall be made only by the Maryland Department of
7 Health to:

8 (i) establish a 24–hour hotline for compulsive and problem gamblers
9 and to provide counseling and other support services for compulsive and problem gamblers;

10 (ii) establish an outreach program for compulsive and problem
11 gamblers, including individuals who requested placement on the voluntary exclusion list
12 established by the Commission under § 9–1A–24 of this subtitle, for the purpose of
13 participating in problem gambling treatment and prevention programs;

14 (iii) develop and implement free or reduced cost problem gambling
15 treatment and prevention programs, including the programs established under Title 19,
16 Subtitle 8 of the Health – General Article; [and]

17 (iv) develop and implement free or reduced cost problem gambling
18 treatment and prevention programs targeted at individuals with problem gambling issues
19 related to sports wagering, participation in fantasy competitions, and other forms of
20 wagering, whether legal or illegal, conducted in the State or through online means; AND

21 (V) PROVIDE REIMBURSEMENT TO SUPPORT PEERS PROVIDING
22 BEHAVIORAL HEALTH SERVICES TO INDIVIDUALS WITH PROBLEM GAMBLING ISSUES
23 WHO ARE RECEIVING TREATMENT OR PARTICIPATING IN PREVENTION PROGRAMS
24 DESCRIBED UNDER ITEMS (III) AND (IV) OF THIS PARAGRAPH.

25 (5) After satisfying the requirements of paragraph (4) of this subsection,
26 any unspent funds in the Problem Gambling Fund may be expended by the Maryland
27 Department of Health on drug and other addiction treatment services.

28 (6) Expenditures from the Problem Gambling Fund shall be made in
29 accordance with an appropriation approved by the General Assembly in the annual State
30 budget or by the budget amendment procedure provided for in § 7–209 of the State Finance
