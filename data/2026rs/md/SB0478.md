A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Washington County – Institutions of Higher Education – Dually Enrolled
3 Students

4 FOR the purpose of altering the definition of “dually enrolled student” to include both a
5 nonpublic secondary school student and a homeschool student in Washington County
6 so that these students are eligible to apply for the Part–Time Grant Program and
7 the Early College Access Grant; and generally relating to institutions of higher
8 education and dually enrolled students.
