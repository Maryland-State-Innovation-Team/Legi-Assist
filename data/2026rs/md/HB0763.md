A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Speed Monitoring Systems – Citations – Failure to Pay

3 FOR the purpose of repealing a requirement that a citation issued for a violation recorded
4 by a speed monitoring system include information advising that a failure to pay or
5 contest liability is an admission of liability; and generally relating to citations and
6 speed monitoring systems.
