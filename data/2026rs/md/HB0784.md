A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Aquaculture – Placement of Shellfish, Bags, Nets, and Structures on Submerged
3 Aquatic Vegetation – Extension

4 FOR the purpose of extending the authorization of a leaseholder of certain aquaculture
5 leases, with prior written approval from the Department of Natural Resources, to
6 place shellfish, bags, nets, and structures on submerged aquatic vegetation, subject
7 to certain requirements; and generally relating to aquaculture leases and submerged
8 aquatic vegetation.

12 (2) Shall authorize for water column leases the placement of shellfish,

15 (3) Shall authorize harvest by diving in areas on any submerged land lease
16 where submerged aquatic vegetation is present.
