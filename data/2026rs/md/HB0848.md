A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Safety – State Fire Prevention Code – Trash and Recyclable Materials

3 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Fire Prevention Commission to adopt regulations
4 that incorporate by reference certain provisions of the National Fire Prevention
5 Association Life Safety Code in the State Fire Prevention Code and authorize certain
6 trash and recyclable materials to be placed in certain locations in accordance with
7 the regulations; and generally relating to the State Fire Prevention Code.

8 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
9 Article – Public Safety
10 Section 6–206(a)
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

15 Article – Public Safety

16 6–206.

17 (a) (1) (i) To protect life and property from the hazards of fire and
18 explosion, the Commission shall adopt comprehensive regulations as a State Fire
19 Prevention Code.

20 (ii) The State Fire Prevention Code shall comply with standard safe
21 practice as embodied in widely recognized standards of good practice for fire prevention and
22 fire protection.

23 (iii) The State Fire Prevention Code has the force and effect of law in
24 the political subdivisions of the State.
