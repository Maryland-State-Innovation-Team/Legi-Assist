A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Election Law – Certification of Election–Supporting Technology – Regulations,
3 Review, and Evaluation

4 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Board of Elections to adopt regulations for the
5 review, certification, and decertification of election–supporting technology and to
6 review and evaluate election–supporting technology on or before a certain date each
7 year; and generally relating to the certification of election–supporting technology.

8 BY adding to
9 Article – Election Law
10 Section 9–107
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

15 Article – Election Law

16 9–107.

17 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION, “ELECTION–SUPPORTING TECHNOLOGY”
18 MEANS ANY EQUIPMENT OR TECHNOLOGY THAT IS DESIGNED FOR USE IN THE
19 ADMINISTRATION OF ELECTIONS, INCLUDING:

20 (I) ELECTRONIC POLLBOOKS;

21 (II) RISK–LIMITING AUDIT TOOLS; AND

22 (III) ANY SOFTWARE USED FOR PREPARING, PRESENTING, OR
