A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Health Occupations – Licensed Psychologists – Prescriptive Authority

3 FOR the purpose of authorizing a licensed psychologist to use prescriptive authority under
4 certain circumstances if the psychologist is certified by the State Board of Examiners
5 of Psychologists; establishing the Prescriptive Authority Advisory Committee to
6 advise the Board on oversight and implementation of certain provisions of this Act;
7 establishing application and qualification requirements for certified prescribing
8 psychologists; establishing requirements for prescribing psychologists who use
9 prescriptive authority; and generally relating to licensed psychologists and
10 prescriptive authority.

11 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
12 Article – Health Occupations
13 Section 12–101(a), 18–101(a), and 18–302(a) through (f) and (h)(1)
