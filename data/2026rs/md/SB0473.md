A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Utilities – For–Hire Drivers and Transportation Network Operators –
3 Human Trafficking Awareness and Prevention Training and Liability

4 FOR the purpose of requiring an applicant for certain for–hire driver’s licenses and
5 transportation network operator’s licenses to submit to the Public Service
6 Commission documentation verifying that the applicant completed a certain human
7 trafficking awareness training program; prohibiting licensed taxicab drivers,
8 for–hire drivers, or transportation network operators from being held civilly or
9 criminally liable for reporting or responding to in good faith a suspected incident of
10 human trafficking; and generally relating to for–hire drivers and transportation
11 network operators.
