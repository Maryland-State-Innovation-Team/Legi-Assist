1 (c) A graduate and professional scholarship award for a single school year may
2 not be less than $1,000 or more than $5,000.

3 (d) A recipient may not receive an award for longer than 8 semesters.

4 (e) (1) The Commission shall allocate grants for the Program to the
5 institutions of higher education specified in subsection (b)(3) of this section based on the
6 proportion of State residents enrolled in eligible programs at each institution.

7 (2) An institution of higher education that receives a grant under this
8 section shall provide the Commission with an annual audit describing the disposition of the
9 funds.

10 (f) The Commission shall:

11 (1) Establish guidelines for the awarding of graduate and professional
12 scholarships by the institutions of higher education to the students; and

13 (2) Adopt regulations necessary for the implementation of this section.

14 (g) Funds for the Maryland Graduate and Professional Scholarship Program shall
15 be as provided in the State budget.
