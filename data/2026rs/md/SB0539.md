A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Department of Social and Economic Mobility – Workforce Opportunities Grant
3 Program and Fund

4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Workforce Opportunities Grant Program to provide
5 grants to certain organizations to support the planning and execution of workforce
6 events and job fairs in the State; establishing the Workforce Opportunities Grant
7 Fund as a special, nonlapsing fund to support the Program; requiring that interest
8 earnings be credited to the Fund; and generally relating to the Workforce
9 Opportunities Grant Program and Fund.

5 PART IV. WORKFORCE OPPORTUNITIES GRANT PROGRAM.

6 9–4125.

7 (A) IN THIS PART THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
8 INDICATED.

9 (B) “FUND” MEANS THE WORKFORCE OPPORTUNITIES GRANT FUND.

10 (C) “PROGRAM” MEANS THE WORKFORCE OPPORTUNITIES GRANT
11 PROGRAM.

12 9–4126.

13 (A) THERE IS A WORKFORCE OPPORTUNITIES GRANT PROGRAM IN THE
14 DEPARTMENT.

15 (B) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ADMINISTER THE PROGRAM.

16 (C) THE PURPOSE OF THE PROGRAM IS TO PROVIDE GRANTS TO SUPPORT
17 THE PLANNING AND EXECUTION OF WORKFORCE EVENTS AND JOB FAIRS IN THE
18 STATE THAT CONNECT JOB SEEKERS WITH EMPLOYERS.

19 9–4127.

20 (A) SUBJECT TO THE AVAILABILITY OF FUNDING FOR THE PROGRAM, THE
21 DEPARTMENT SHALL ESTABLISH AN APPLICATION PROCESS THAT AWARDS GRANTS
22 ON A COMPETITIVE BASIS TO NONPROFIT ORGANIZATIONS, LOCAL WORKFORCE
23 BOARDS, AND PARTNERSHIPS THAT INCLUDE EMPLOYERS, COMMUNITY
24 ORGANIZATIONS, AND OTHER STAKEHOLDERS THAT:

25 (1) REGULARLY WORK WITH SMALL AND LARGE EMPLOYERS FROM
26 MULTIPLE INDUSTRIES AND REGIONS ACROSS THE STATE;

1 (2) CAN CONDUCT WORKFORCE EVENTS OR JOB FAIRS IN MULTIPLE
2 REGIONS OF THE STATE THAT ARE ACCESSIBLE TO JOB SEEKERS AND EMPLOYERS;

3 (3) HAVE EXPERIENCE ORGANIZING EVENTS AND WORKING WITH
4 EMPLOYERS WHO HAVE THE ABILITY TO HIRE JOB SEEKERS; AND

5 (4) HAVE THE CAPACITY TO CONNECT JOB SEEKERS DIRECTLY WITH

13 (5) STAFFING; AND

14 (6) EVENT COORDINATION.

15 9–4128.

16 (A) THERE IS A WORKFORCE OPPORTUNITIES GRANT FUND.

17 (B) THE PURPOSE OF THE FUND IS TO SUPPORT THE PROGRAM.

20 SUBJECT TO § 7–302 OF THE STATE FINANCE AND PROCUREMENT ARTICLE.

21 (2) THE STATE TREASURER SHALL HOLD THE FUND SEPARATELY,
22 AND THE COMPTROLLER SHALL ACCOUNT FOR THE FUND.

24 (1) MONEY APPROPRIATED IN THE STATE BUDGET TO THE FUND;

25 (2) INTEREST EARNINGS; AND
