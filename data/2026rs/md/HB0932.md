A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Maryland Transit Administration – Fifth Bus Division Facility – Construction

3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Transit Administration to complete
4 construction of the Administration’s fifth bus division facility on or before a certain
5 date; and generally relating to the Maryland Transit Administration’s fifth bus
6 division.

7 BY adding to
8 Article – Transportation
9 Section 7–720
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2020 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

14 Article – Transportation

15 7–720.

16 (A) ON OR BEFORE JUNE 30, 2032, THE ADMINISTRATION SHALL
17 COMPLETE CONSTRUCTION OF A FIFTH BUS DIVISION FACILITY.

18 (B) BEGINNING WITH THE FISCAL YEAR 2027 THROUGH 2033
19 CONSOLIDATED TRANSPORTATION PROGRAM, THE DEPARTMENT AND THE
20 ADMINISTRATION SHALL IDENTIFY THE FUNDING NECESSARY TO ENSURE THAT THE
21 ADMINISTRATION’S FIFTH BUS DIVISION FACILITY IS IN OPERATION ON OR BEFORE
22 JUNE 30, 2032.
