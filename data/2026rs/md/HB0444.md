CHAPTER ______

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Safety – Immigration Enforcement Agreements – Prohibition

3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting the State, a unit of local government, a county sheriff, or
4 any agency, officer, employee, or agent of the State or a unit of local government from
5 entering into a certain immigration enforcement agreement; requiring the
6 termination of an existing immigration enforcement agreement on or before a certain
7 date; and generally relating to immigration enforcement.

8 BY adding to
9 Article – Criminal Procedure
10 Section 5–104.1

1 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
2 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

3 Article – Criminal Procedure

4 5–104.1.

5 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION, “IMMIGRATION ENFORCEMENT AGREEMENT”
6 MEANS A CONTRACT, AN AGREEMENT, AN INTERGOVERNMENTAL SERVICE
7 AGREEMENT, OR A MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING WITH THE FEDERAL
8 GOVERNMENT THAT AUTHORIZES THE STATE, A UNIT OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT, A
9 COUNTY SHERIFF, OR ANY AGENCY, OFFICER, EMPLOYEE, OR AGENT OF THE STATE
10 OR A UNIT OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT TO ENFORCE CIVIL IMMIGRATION LAW.

11 (2) “IMMIGRATION ENFORCEMENT AGREEMENT” INCLUDES AN
12 AGREEMENT MADE IN ACCORDANCE WITH:

13 (I) 8 U.S.C. § 1103;

14 (II) 8 U.S.C. § 1357; OR

15 (III) ANY OTHER FEDERAL LAW.

16 (B) THE STATE, A UNIT OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT, A COUNTY SHERIFF, OR
17 ANY AGENCY, OFFICER, EMPLOYEE, OR AGENT OF THE STATE OR A UNIT OF LOCAL
18 GOVERNMENT MAY NOT ENTER INTO AN IMMIGRATION ENFORCEMENT AGREEMENT.

19 (C) THE STATE, A UNIT OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT, A COUNTY SHERIFF, OR
20 ANY AGENCY, OFFICER, EMPLOYEE, OR AGENT OF THE STATE OR A UNIT OF LOCAL
21 GOVERNMENT WITH AN EXISTING IMMIGRATION ENFORCEMENT AGREEMENT SHALL
22 EXERCISE THE TERMINATION PROVISION CONTAINED IN THE IMMIGRATION
23 ENFORCEMENT AGREEMENT NOT LATER THAN JULY 1, 2026.
