1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Schools – School Resource Officers – Firearms Required

3 FOR the purpose of repealing a certain provision of law relating to a certain school police
4 officer’s authorization to carry a firearm under certain circumstances; requiring a
5 school resource officer to carry a firearm while present on the premises of a certain
6 school; and generally relating to school resource officers.
