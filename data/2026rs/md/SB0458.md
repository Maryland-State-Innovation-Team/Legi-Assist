A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Vehicle Laws – Crosswalk Monitoring Systems – Authorization

3 FOR the purpose of authorizing the use of crosswalk monitoring systems in school zones if
4 authorized by local law; providing that the owner or driver of a motor vehicle
5 recorded failing to yield to a pedestrian crossing a roadway in a crosswalk is subject
6 to a citation and a certain civil penalty under certain circumstances; establishing
7 certain defenses to a charge of an alleged violation recorded by a crosswalk
8 monitoring system; prohibiting a contractor administering a crosswalk monitoring
9 system from being compensated in a certain manner; and generally relating to the
10 use of crosswalk monitoring systems.
