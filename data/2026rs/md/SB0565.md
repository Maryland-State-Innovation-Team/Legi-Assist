A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Maryland Stadium Authority – Sale of Shamrock Farms to Carroll County

3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting the Maryland Stadium Authority from selling, transferring,
4 or disposing of Shamrock Farms except under certain conditions; authorizing the
5 County Commissioners of Carroll County to purchase Shamrock Farms on or before
6 a certain date subject to certain terms, conditions, and notice requirements; and
7 generally relating to the sale of Shamrock Farms.
