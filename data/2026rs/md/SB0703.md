A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Caroline County – Regulation of Animals – Dogs

3 FOR the purpose of repealing certain requirements for the issuance of dog licenses in
4 Caroline County; and generally relating to the regulation of dogs in Caroline County.

5 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
6 Article – Local Government
7 Section 13–116
8 Annotated Code of Maryland
9 (2013 Volume and 2025 Supplement)

10 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
11 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

12 Article – Local Government

13 13–116.

14 (a) [(1) In Caroline County, on or before July 1 of each year, a person owning
15 or keeping a dog shall apply to the county tax collector for a license for the dog if the dog is
16 at least 6 months old.

17 (2) At the time of application, the applicant shall pay the fee for a dog or
18 kennel license set by the county commissioners.

19 (3) Except as provided in § 13–108 of this subtitle, the licenses and fees
20 required under this section are the only licenses and fees required for owning or keeping a
21 dog.
