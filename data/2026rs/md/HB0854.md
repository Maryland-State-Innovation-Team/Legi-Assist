A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 School Construction – Nonpublic Special Education School Renovation
3 Program – Established

4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Nonpublic Special Education School Renovation
5 Program in the Interagency Commission on School Construction to provide grant
6 funding to certain nonpublic special education schools for certain renovations and
7 improvements; and generally relating to the Nonpublic Special Education School
8 Renovation Program.

9 BY adding to
10 Article – Education
11 Section 5–331
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

16 Article – Education

17 5–331.

18 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “PROGRAM” MEANS THE NONPUBLIC SPECIAL
19 EDUCATION SCHOOL RENOVATION PROGRAM.

20 (B) (1) THERE IS A NONPUBLIC SPECIAL EDUCATION SCHOOL
21 RENOVATION PROGRAM.

22 (2) THE PURPOSE OF THE PROGRAM IS TO PROVIDE GRANTS TO
23 NONPUBLIC SPECIAL EDUCATION SCHOOLS FOR:

1 (I) RENOVATIONS AND IMPROVEMENTS TO CLASSROOMS AND

3 (II) UPGRADES TO RESIDENTIAL COTTAGES ON SCHOOL
4 GROUNDS THAT WILL IMPROVE STUDENT TREATMENT AND SAFETY;

15 (2) A NONPUBLIC SPECIAL EDUCATION SCHOOL IS ELIGIBLE FOR THE
16 PROGRAM IF THE SCHOOL:

17 (I) OPERATES IN CONJUNCTION WITH A RESIDENTIAL
18 TREATMENT CENTER THAT IS LICENSED BY THE MARYLAND DEPARTMENT OF
19 HEALTH, THE DEPARTMENT OF HUMAN SERVICES, OR THE DEPARTMENT OF
20 JUVENILE SERVICES;

21 (II) PROVIDES:

22 1. HIGH SCHOOL CREDIT TOWARDS THE HIGH SCHOOL
23 GRADUATION REQUIREMENTS APPROVED BY THE DEPARTMENT; OR

24 2. A CURRICULUM AND EDUCATIONAL PROGRAM FOR
25 ELEMENTARY OR MIDDLE SCHOOL STUDENTS THAT IS APPROVED BY THE
26 DEPARTMENT; AND

27 (III) PROVIDES SERVICES FOR STUDENTS PLACED IN
28 NONPUBLIC EDUCATIONAL PROGRAMS UNDER § 8–406 OF THIS ARTICLE.

29 (E) THE INTERAGENCY COMMISSION MAY PRIORITIZE APPLICATIONS FOR:
