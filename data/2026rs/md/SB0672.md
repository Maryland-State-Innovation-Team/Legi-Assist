A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Prince George’s County – Expanding Access to Early Childhood Education and
3 Child Care

4 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Department of Education to develop an
5 implementation and financing plan to expand access to high–quality early childhood
6 education and child care in Prince George’s County for children from birth through
7 3 years; requiring the Department to consult with certain entities; requiring the plan
8 to include certain analyses; and generally relating to early childhood education and
9 child care in Prince George’s County.

10 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
11 That:

12 (a) The State Department of Education shall develop an implementation and
13 financing plan to expand access to high–quality early childhood education and child care in
14 Prince George’s County for children from birth through 3 years.

15 (b) To complete the plan, the Department shall consult with Prince George’s
16 County and relevant State agencies.

17 (c) The plan shall include an analysis of:

18 (1) the cost to parents, the county, and the State;

19 (2) the workforce needs of early childhood education and child care
20 providers to expand access;

21 (3) the capacity of early childhood education and child care providers to
22 serve more children; and

23 (4) additional county and State revenue options to expand access.
