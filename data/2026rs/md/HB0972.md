A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Maryland Fair and Agricultural Education Promise Fund – Establishment
3 (Maryland Fair and Agricultural Education Promise Act)

4 FOR the purpose of establishing the Maryland Fair and Agricultural Education Promise
5 Fund as a special, nonlapsing fund to provide grants that advance agricultural fairs
6 and education in the State; requiring interest earnings of the Fund to be credited to
7 the Fund; requiring a certain amount of proceeds from the State lottery, before being
8 allocated to the General Fund of the State, to be allocated to the Fund; repealing the
9 requirement that a certain amount of annual proceeds from the State horse racing
10 Special Fund be used to promote State agricultural fairs and education; and
11 generally relating to the Maryland Fair and Agricultural Education Promise Fund.

1 (A) THERE IS A MARYLAND FAIR AND AGRICULTURAL EDUCATION
2 PROMISE FUND.

3 (B) THE PURPOSE OF THE FUND IS TO PROVIDE GRANTS THAT ADVANCE
4 AGRICULTURAL FAIRS AND EDUCATION IN THE STATE.

5 (C) THE COMPTROLLER SHALL ADMINISTER THE FUND.

6 (D) (1) THE FUND IS A SPECIAL, NONLAPSING FUND THAT IS NOT
7 SUBJECT TO § 7–302 OF THE STATE FINANCE AND PROCUREMENT ARTICLE.

8 (2) THE STATE TREASURER SHALL HOLD THE FUND SEPARATELY,
9 AND THE COMPTROLLER SHALL ACCOUNT FOR THE FUND.

10 (E) THE FUND CONSISTS OF:

11 (1) REVENUE DISTRIBUTED TO THE FUND UNDER § 9–120(B)(1)(XV)
12 OF THE STATE GOVERNMENT ARTICLE;

13 (2) MONEY APPROPRIATED IN THE STATE BUDGET TO THE FUND;

14 (3) INTEREST EARNINGS; AND

15 (4) ANY OTHER MONEY FROM ANY OTHER SOURCE ACCEPTED FOR
16 THE BENEFIT OF THE FUND.

17 (F) THE FUND MAY BE USED ONLY:

18 (1) TO PROVIDE ANNUAL GRANTS IN ACCORDANCE WITH § 10–2203
19 OF THIS SUBTITLE; AND

20 (2) FOR ADMINISTRATIVE EXPENSES OF THE COMPTROLLER IN
21 CARRYING OUT THIS SUBTITLE.

22 (G) (1) THE STATE TREASURER SHALL INVEST THE MONEY OF THE FUND
23 IN THE SAME MANNER AS OTHER STATE MONEY MAY BE INVESTED.

24 (2) ANY INTEREST EARNINGS OF THE FUND SHALL BE CREDITED TO
25 THE FUND.

26 (H) EXPENDITURES FROM THE FUND MAY BE MADE ONLY IN ACCORDANCE
27 WITH THE STATE BUDGET.
