A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Prekindergarten Programs and Primary and Secondary Schools – Student
3 Privacy Policy Requirements and Discrimination Reporting

4 FOR the purpose of requiring public and nonpublic primary or secondary schools to adopt
5 certain policies and procedures for protecting the privacy of student records;
6 requiring nonpublic prekindergarten programs and nonpublic primary and
7 secondary schools to report certain information about certain incidents of alleged
8 discrimination to the State Department of Education annually; and generally
9 relating to student privacy and discrimination in education.

2 7–137.

3 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “SCHOOL” MEANS:

4 (1) A PUBLIC PRIMARY OR SECONDARY SCHOOL; OR

5 (2) A NONPUBLIC PRIMARY OR SECONDARY SCHOOL.

6 (B) A SCHOOL SHALL ADOPT WRITTEN POLICIES AND PROCEDURES ON
7 PROTECTING THE RIGHT OF PRIVACY OF A STUDENT AND A STUDENT’S PARENT OR
8 GUARDIAN REGARDING ACCESS TO AND THE RELEASE OF THE STUDENT’S RECORDS.

9 (C) THE WRITTEN POLICIES AND PROCEDURES UNDER SUBSECTION (B) OF
10 THIS SECTION SHALL INCLUDE THE FOLLOWING:

11 (1) A POLICY STATEMENT ASSURING THAT ALL RECORDS
12 PERTAINING TO A STUDENT ARE AVAILABLE TO THE STUDENT’S PARENT OR
13 GUARDIAN FOR INSPECTION OR REVIEW;

14 (2) PROCEDURES TO BE FOLLOWED BY A PARENT OR GUARDIAN WHO
15 REQUESTS TO INSPECT OR REVIEW THE RECORDS OF THE PARENT OR GUARDIAN’S
16 CHILD;

17 (3) A POLICY STATEMENT ASSURING THAT THE SCHOOL MAY NOT
18 DISCLOSE PERSONAL IDENTIFYING INFORMATION FROM A STUDENT’S RECORD
19 WITHOUT PRIOR WRITTEN CONSENT OF THE STUDENT’S PARENT OR GUARDIAN
20 UNLESS OTHERWISE ALLOWED BY LAW;

21 (4) PROCEDURES BY WHICH THE SCHOOL SHALL MAINTAIN A
22 WRITTEN RECORD OF REQUESTS FOR, AND DISCLOSURE OF, PERSONAL
23 IDENTIFYING INFORMATION FROM A STUDENT’S RECORD THAT INCLUDES:

24 1. THE FIRST, MIDDLE, AND LAST NAME OF THE STUDENT;

25 2. THE NAME OF THE REVIEWER;

26 3. THE MONTH, DAY, AND YEAR OF THE REVIEW; AND

27 4. THE PURPOSE OF THE REVIEW; AND
