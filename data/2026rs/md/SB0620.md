A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Criminal Law – Wearing, Carrying, or Transporting a Handgun – Prior
3 Convictions

4 FOR the purpose of altering the list of convictions that subject a defendant to enhanced
5 sentencing for wearing, carrying, or transporting a handgun to include certain
6 extra–jurisdictional crimes; and generally relating to wearing, carrying, or
7 transporting a handgun.
