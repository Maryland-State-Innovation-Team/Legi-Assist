A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Health – Local Suicide Fatality Review Teams – Authorization

3 FOR the purpose of authorizing a county or municipality to establish a local suicide fatality
4 review team; requiring a local team to coordinate with the State Suicide Fatality
5 Review Committee; exempting certain meetings of a local team from the Open
6 Meetings Act; providing that certain information acquired by a local team is
7 confidential and exempt from disclosure under the Maryland Public Information Act;
8 prohibiting certain persons from testifying in any proceeding about details of a local
9 team meeting; and generally relating to local suicide fatality review teams.

10 BY adding to
11 Article – Health – General
12 Section 5–1101 through 5–1105 to be under the new subtitle “Subtitle 11. Local
13 Suicide Fatality Review Teams”
14 Annotated Code of Maryland
15 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

16 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
17 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

18 Article – Health – General

19 SUBTITLE 11. LOCAL SUICIDE FATALITY REVIEW TEAMS.

20 5–1101.

21 IN THIS SUBTITLE, “LOCAL TEAM” MEANS A MULTIDISCIPLINARY AND
22 MULTIAGENCY SUICIDE FATALITY REVIEW TEAM ESTABLISHED UNDER § 5–1102 OF
23 THIS SUBTITLE.

10 5–1103.

11 (A) THE PURPOSE OF A LOCAL TEAM IS TO PREVENT SUICIDE DEATHS BY:

12 (1) REVIEWING SUICIDE DEATHS THAT OCCUR WITHIN THE COUNTY;

13 (2) IDENTIFYING SYSTEMIC, SERVICE, AND POLICY FACTORS
14 ASSOCIATED WITH RISK OF SUICIDE;

15 (3) PROMOTING COORDINATION BETWEEN ENTITIES INVOLVED IN
16 SUICIDE PREVENTION, CRISIS RESPONSE, TREATMENT, AND POSTVENTION; AND

17 (4) DEVELOPING RECOMMENDATIONS TO PREVENT SUICIDE.

18 (B) A LOCAL TEAM MAY:

19 (1) EXAMINE TRENDS IN SUICIDE AND FACTORS THAT CONTRIBUTE
20 TO SUICIDE RISK;

21 (2) IDENTIFY GAPS IN SYSTEMS OF CARE OR COORDINATION
22 BETWEEN ENTITIES INVOLVED IN SUICIDE PREVENTION, CRISIS RESPONSE,
23 TREATMENT, AND POSTVENTION; AND

24 (3) SUBMIT RECOMMENDATIONS TO THE STATE SUICIDE FATALITY
25 REVIEW COMMITTEE ESTABLISHED UNDER SUBTITLE 10 OF THIS TITLE, OR OTHER
26 LOCAL ENTITIES AS DETERMINED APPROPRIATE BY THE LOCAL TEAM.

1 (A) MEETINGS OF LOCAL TEAMS SHALL BE CLOSED TO THE PUBLIC AND ARE
2 NOT SUBJECT TO THE MARYLAND OPEN MEETINGS ACT WHEN THE LOCAL TEAMS
3 ARE DISCUSSING INDIVIDUAL CASES OF SUICIDE.

4 (B) EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN SUBSECTION (C) OF THIS SECTION, MEETINGS
5 OF LOCAL TEAMS SHALL BE OPEN TO THE PUBLIC AND ARE SUBJECT TO THE
6 MARYLAND OPEN MEETINGS ACT WHEN THE LOCAL TEAM IS NOT DISCUSSING
7 INDIVIDUAL CASES OF SUICIDE.

8 (C) (1) DURING A PUBLIC MEETING, INFORMATION MAY NOT BE
9 DISCLOSED THAT IDENTIFIES:

10 (I) A DECEASED INDIVIDUAL;

11 (II) AN INDIVIDUAL WHO HAS EXPERIENCED SUICIDE OR A
12 SUICIDE ATTEMPT;

13 (III) A FAMILY MEMBER, GUARDIAN, OR CARETAKER OF AN
14 INDIVIDUAL DESCRIBED IN ITEM (I) OR (II) OF THIS PARAGRAPH; OR

15 (IV) AN INDIVIDUAL CONVICTED OF A CRIME OR ADJUDICATED
16 AS HAVING COMMITTED A DELINQUENT ACT THAT CAUSED A DEATH OR NEAR
17 FATALITY.

18 (2) DURING A PUBLIC MEETING, INFORMATION MAY NOT BE
19 DISCLOSED ABOUT THE INVOLVEMENT OF AN AGENCY WITH:

20 (I) A DECEASED INDIVIDUAL;

21 (II) AN INDIVIDUAL WHO HAS EXPERIENCED SUICIDE OR A
22 SUICIDE ATTEMPT;

23 (III) A FAMILY MEMBER, GUARDIAN, OR CARETAKER OF A
24 DECEASED INDIVIDUAL OR OF AN INDIVIDUAL DESCRIBED IN ITEM (I) OR (II) OF THIS
25 PARAGRAPH; OR

26 (IV) AN INDIVIDUAL CONVICTED OF A CRIME OR ADJUDICATED
27 AS HAVING COMMITTED A DELINQUENT ACT THAT CAUSED A DEATH OR NEAR
28 FATALITY.

29 (D) THIS SECTION DOES NOT PROHIBIT A LOCAL TEAM FROM REQUESTING
30 THE ATTENDANCE OF A PERSON WHO HAS INFORMATION RELEVANT TO THE LOCAL
31 TEAM’S EXERCISE OF ITS PURPOSE AND DUTIES AT A TEAM MEETING.
