1 (2) THERE IS AN AGING RESILIENCE FUND.

2 (3) THE PURPOSE OF THE FUND IS TO SUPPORT THE
3 IMPLEMENTATION OF THE DEPARTMENT’S MISSION UNDER THIS TITLE.

4 (4) THE SECRETARY SHALL ADMINISTER THE FUND.

5 (5) (I) THE FUND IS A SPECIAL, NONLAPSING FUND THAT IS NOT
6 SUBJECT TO § 7–302 OF THE STATE FINANCE AND PROCUREMENT ARTICLE.

7 (II) THE STATE TREASURER SHALL HOLD THE FUND
8 SEPARATELY, AND THE COMPTROLLER SHALL ACCOUNT FOR THE FUND.

9 (6) THE FUND CONSISTS OF:

10 (I) INVESTMENT EARNINGS OF THE FUND;

11 (II) ANY FEES COLLECTED UNDER THIS SUBTITLE; AND

12 (III) ANY OTHER MONEY FROM ANY OTHER SOURCE ACCEPTED
13 FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE FUND.

14 (7) THE FUND MAY BE USED FOR ADMINISTRATIVE EXPENSES THAT
15 ALIGN WITH THE DEPARTMENT’S MISSION UNDER THIS TITLE, INCLUDING:

16 (I) PERSONNEL;

17 (II) PARTNERSHIP DEVELOPMENT; AND

18 (III) ANY PROGRAMS, SERVICES, OR ACTIVITIES WITHIN THE
19 DEPARTMENT.

20 (8) (I) THE STATE TREASURER SHALL INVEST THE MONEY OF THE
21 FUND IN THE SAME MANNER AS OTHER STATE MONEY MAY BE INVESTED.

22 (II) ANY INTEREST EARNINGS OF THE FUND SHALL BE
23 CREDITED TO THE FUND.

24 (9) EXPENDITURES FROM THE FUND MAY BE MADE ONLY IN
25 ACCORDANCE WITH THE STATE BUDGET.
