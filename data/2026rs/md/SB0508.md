4 Board of Education regarding student members, appointments to fill certain

16 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, in accordance with Article
17 III, § 35 of the Maryland Constitution, this Act may not be construed to extend or apply to
18 the salary or compensation of a member of the Somerset County Board of Education while
19 serving in a term of office beginning before the effective date of this Act, but the provisions
20 of this Act concerning the salary or compensation of the members of the Somerset County
21 Board of Education shall take effect at the beginning of the next following term of office.
22 This limitation does not apply to an individual appointed or elected after the effective date
23 of this Act to fill out an unexpired term.

27 each of the two Houses of the General Assembly, and shall take effect from the date it is
28 enacted.
