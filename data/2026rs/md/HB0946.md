1 (a) (1) In this [section] SUBTITLE the following words have the meanings

3 (3) “Office” means the Appointments Office in the Office of the Governor
4 that performs the function of recommending to the Governor the appointment or
5 nomination of an individual to serve as a member of a State or local board, commission,
6 council, committee, authority, task force, or other entity that by law requires the
7 membership to be appointed in whole or in part by the Governor, whether or not the
8 appointment or nomination is with the advice and consent of the Senate or House of
9 Delegates.

10 8–3A–03.

11 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “JUDGE” HAS THE MEANING STATED IN § 1–101 OF
12 THE COURTS ARTICLE.

13 (B) ON OR BEFORE DECEMBER 1, 2026, AND EACH DECEMBER 1
14 THEREAFTER, THE OFFICE SHALL REPORT TO THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY, IN
15 ACCORDANCE WITH § 2–1257 OF THIS ARTICLE, AND MAKE PUBLICLY AVAILABLE A
16 REPORT FOR THE PRIOR CALENDAR YEAR ON:

17 (1) THE NUMBER OF INDIVIDUALS WHO APPLY TO A JUDICIAL
18 NOMINATING COMMISSION SEEKING APPOINTMENT AS A JUDGE, DISAGGREGATED
19 BY THE JUDICIAL OFFICE SOUGHT, ETHNICITY, GENDER IDENTITY, DISABILITY
20 STATUS, VETERAN STATUS, AND SEXUAL ORIENTATION;

21 (2) THE NUMBER OF INDIVIDUALS RECOMMENDED BY A JUDICIAL
22 NOMINATING COMMISSION TO THE OFFICE FOR APPOINTMENT AS A JUDGE,
23 DISAGGREGATED BY THE JUDICIAL OFFICE FOR WHICH THE INDIVIDUAL WAS
24 RECOMMENDED, ETHNICITY, GENDER IDENTITY, DISABILITY STATUS, VETERAN
25 STATUS, AND SEXUAL ORIENTATION; AND

26 (3) THE NUMBER OF INDIVIDUALS NOMINATED BY THE GOVERNOR TO
27 SERVE AS A JUDGE, DISAGGREGATED BY THE JUDICIAL OFFICE TO WHICH THE
28 INDIVIDUAL WAS NOMINATED, ETHNICITY, GENDER IDENTITY, DISABILITY STATUS,
29 VETERAN STATUS, AND SEXUAL ORIENTATION.

30 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
