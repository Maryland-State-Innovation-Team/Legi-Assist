A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Frederick Community College – Board of Trustees – Meeting and Training
3 Requirements

4 FOR the purpose of establishing certain public comment requirements and recording
5 requirements for the Board of Community College Trustees for Frederick County;
6 requiring the members of the Board to undergo certain training; and generally
7 relating to the Board of Community College Trustees for Frederick County.

8 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
9 Article – Education
10 Section 16–407
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

15 Article – Education

16 16–407.

17 (a) The Board of Community College Trustees for Frederick County consists of
18 seven members. Of the members:

19 (1) Each shall be a resident of Frederick County; and

20 (2) Each shall be appointed by the Governor, with the advice and consent
21 of the Senate, for a term of 5 years.

22 (b) (1) A member of the Board of Community College Trustees may not serve
23 more than three consecutive 5–year terms.

1 (2) A member of the Frederick County Board of Education may not serve

3 (c) Each member of the Board is entitled to $500 a year for expenses.

4 (D) EACH MEETING OF THE BOARD SHALL INCLUDE AN OPPORTUNITY FOR
5 PUBLIC COMMENT.

6 (E) (1) THE BOARD SHALL MAKE A VIDEO OR AUDIO RECORDING OF EACH
7 MEETING OF THE BOARD.

8 (2) THE BOARD SHALL MAKE A RECORDING REQUIRED UNDER
9 PARAGRAPH (1) OF THIS SUBSECTION AVAILABLE TO THE PUBLIC ON THE BOARD’S
10 WEBSITE WITHOUT CHARGE AND, TO THE EXTENT PRACTICABLE, WITHIN 2
11 BUSINESS DAYS AFTER THE RECORDING IS MADE.

12 (F) AS PART OF ONGOING TRAINING FOR BOARD MEMBERS AND AT LEAST
13 EVERY 2 YEARS, THE BOARD MEMBERS SHALL UNDERGO TRAINING SPECIFIC TO
14 BOARD GOVERNANCE, STRUCTURE, RESPONSIBILITIES, AND ROLES.

15 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect June
