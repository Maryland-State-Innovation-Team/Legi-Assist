1 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
2 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

3 Article – Public Safety

4 14–110.5.

5 (a) (1) In this section the following words have the meanings indicated.

6 (2) “Fund” means the State Disaster Recovery Fund.

7 (3) “Local Disaster Declaration” means a formal request for State disaster
8 relief and recovery assistance made as a result of an emergency by the senior elected official
9 through the local organization for emergency management.

10 (f) (1) The Governor may include in the annual budget bill an appropriation
11 to the Fund.

12 (2) The Fund shall consist of:

13 (i) money appropriated in the State budget to the Fund;

14 (ii) money transferred to the Fund from:

15 1. the Catastrophic Event Account in accordance with §
16 7–324 of the State Finance and Procurement Article; AND

17 2. THE REVENUE STABILIZATION ACCOUNT IN
18 ACCORDANCE WITH § 7–311 OF THE STATE FINANCE AND PROCUREMENT ARTICLE;

19 (iii) repayments of principal and interest from loans made from the
20 Fund;

21 (iv) reimbursements from the federal government or other legal
22 entities for disaster recovery assistance expenditures made from the Fund;

23 (v) interest earnings of the Fund; and

24 (vi) any other money from any other source accepted for the benefit
25 of the Fund.

26 Article – State Finance and Procurement

27 7–311.

28 (a) (1) In this section the following words have the meanings indicated.
