A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Safety – State Law Enforcement Agencies – Hiring Restriction
3 (ICE Breaker Act)

4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting certain State law enforcement agencies from hiring certain
5 individuals who were hired by a certain federal agency after a certain date; and
6 generally relating to hiring by State law enforcement agencies.

7 BY adding to
8 Article – Public Safety
9 Section 3–535
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

14 Article – Public Safety

15 3–535.

16 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION, “STATE LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCY” MEANS A
17 GOVERNMENTAL POLICE FORCE, SECURITY FORCE, OR LAW ENFORCEMENT
18 ORGANIZATION OF THE STATE THAT BY STATUTE, ORDINANCE, OR COMMON LAW IS
19 AUTHORIZED TO ENFORCE THE GENERAL CRIMINAL LAWS OF THE STATE.

20 (2) “STATE LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCY” INCLUDES:
