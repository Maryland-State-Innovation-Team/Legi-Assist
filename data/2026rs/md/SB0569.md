1 (y) (1) “Process” means an operation or set of operations performed by manual

8 14–4707.

9 (a) A controller may not:

10 (1) Except where the collection or processing is strictly necessary to provide
11 or maintain a specific product or service requested by the consumer to whom the personal
12 data pertains, collect, process, or share sensitive data concerning a consumer;

13 (2) Sell sensitive data;

14 (3) Process personal data in violation of State or federal laws that prohibit
15 unlawful discrimination;

16 (4) Process the personal data of a consumer for the purposes of targeted
17 advertising if the controller knew or should have known that the consumer is under the age
18 of 18 years;

19 (5) Sell the personal data of a consumer if the controller knew or should
20 have known that the consumer is under the age of 18 years;

21 (6) Discriminate against a consumer for exercising a consumer right
22 contained in this subtitle, including denying goods or services, charging different prices or
23 rates for goods or services, or providing a different level of quality of goods or services to
24 the consumer;

25 (7) Collect, process, or transfer personal data or publicly available data in
26 a manner that unlawfully discriminates in or otherwise unlawfully makes unavailable the
27 equal enjoyment of goods or services on the basis of race, color, religion, national origin,
28 sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, or disability, unless the collection, processing, or
29 transfer of personal data is for:

30 (i) The controller’s self–testing to prevent or mitigate unlawful
31 discrimination;

32 (ii) The controller’s diversifying of an applicant, participant, or
33 customer pool; or
