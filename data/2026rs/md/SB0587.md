15 19–112.

1 as determined by the Commission, if the Center continuously meets the standards and

3 (c) The designated Center shall:

4 (1) Develop, coordinate, and implement patient safety initiatives across the
5 State;

6 (2) Be a model for patient safety innovation and implementation in the
7 State;

8 (3) Convene health care providers, patients, and families to:

9 (i) Improve the quality of health care and reduce preventable and
10 avoidable harm; and

11 (ii) Provide safe and equitable health care for State citizens; and

12 (4) Share information relating to best practices among providers and
13 patients in the State.

14 (d) (1) There is a Patient Safety Center Fund.

15 (2) The purpose of the Fund is to subsidize a portion of the costs of the
16 Center so that the Center may perform the duties described under subsection (c) of this
17 section.

18 (3) The Commission shall administer the Fund.

19 (4) (i) The Fund is a special, nonlapsing fund that is not subject to §
20 7–302 of the State Finance and Procurement Article.

21 (ii) The State Treasurer shall hold the Fund separately, and the
22 Comptroller shall account for the Fund.

23 (5) The Fund consists of:

24 (i) Money appropriated in the State budget to the Fund;

25 (ii) Interest earnings; and

26 (iii) Any other money from any other source accepted for the benefit
27 of the Fund.

28 (6) The Fund may be used only to subsidize a portion of the costs of the
29 Center so that the Center may perform the duties described under subsection (c) of this
30 section.
