A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Certificates of Public Convenience and Necessity and Transmission Lines –
3 Notice to Landowners

4 FOR the purpose of requiring a person applying for a certificate of public convenience and
5 necessity for the construction of any transmission line to provide certain notices to
6 certain landowners regarding the construction; establishing that failure to provide a
7 certain notice invalidates a certain hearing and, under certain circumstances, voids
8 an application for a certificate of public convenience and necessity; and generally
9 relating to the construction of transmission lines and applications for certificates of
10 public convenience and necessity.
