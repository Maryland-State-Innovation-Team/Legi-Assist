2 HOUSE BILL 960

1 (A) THERE IS A FEDERAL EMPLOYEE–TO–ENTREPRENEUR PROGRAM IN
2 THE DEPARTMENT.

3 (B) THE PURPOSE OF THE PROGRAM IS TO PROVIDE FORMER OR
4 TRANSITIONING FEDERAL EMPLOYEES WITH TRAINING, TOOLS, AND MENTORSHIP
5 NECESSARY FOR SUCCESSFULLY TRANSITIONING TO ENTREPRENEURSHIP.

6 (C) (1) EACH FISCAL YEAR, THE PROGRAM SHALL SERVE FOUR COHORTS
7 OF 15 INDIVIDUALS.

8 (2) FOR EACH COHORT, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL PROVIDE:

9 (I) SESSIONS ON ENTREPRENEURIAL EXPLORATION AND
10 BUSINESS AND CAPITAL READINESS;

11 (II) ONE–ON–ONE COACHING FOR EACH PARTICIPANT;

12 (III) TRAINING IN BUSINESS PLANNING, COMPLIANCE,
13 MARKETING, BUDGETING, AND PITCH DEVELOPMENT; AND

14 (IV) TOOLS TO CREATE A BUSINESS PLAN AND A CAPITAL
15 READINESS STRATEGY.

16 (D) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL:

17 (1) ESTABLISH APPLICATION PROCEDURES FOR AN INDIVIDUAL TO
18 PARTICIPATE IN THE PROGRAM; AND

19 (2) DEVELOP A CURRICULUM FOR THE PROGRAM.

20 (E) FOR EACH FISCAL YEAR, THE GOVERNOR SHALL INCLUDE IN THE
21 ANNUAL BUDGET BILL AN APPROPRIATION OF $400,000 TO THE DEPARTMENT TO
22 CARRY OUT THE PROGRAM.

23 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
24 October 1, 2026.
