13 (9) one at–large member who is a resident selected by the County
14 Commissioners of Washington County.

15 (b) Each member shall be a registered voter of Washington County who in the
16 previous 4 years has voted in at least two elections, at least one of which was in the
17 gubernatorial election year.

18 (c) On or before March 31, 2016, and on or before March 31 each fourth year
19 thereafter, the County Commissioners of Washington County shall request that each
20 organization listed in subsection (a) of this section recommend an appointee to the county
21 commissioners on or before the following May 15.

22 (d) (1) The County Commissioners of Washington County shall appoint the
23 individual recommended by each organization listed in subsection (a) of this section.

24 (2) If an organization fails to make a recommendation to the county
25 commissioners on or before June 1, the members who have been appointed to the
26 Commission shall meet, solicit prospective members from the public, and select by majority
27 vote a qualified substitute public member to the Commission to serve through the following
28 December 1.

29 (3) A member of the Commission may not be an elected official or an
30 employee of an official whose salary the Commission studies.

31 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
32 October 1, 2026.
