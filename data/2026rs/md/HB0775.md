A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Wetlands and Waterways Permits – Lighthouse Piers – Permission of Owner of
3 Private Wetlands

4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a person from being required to obtain permission from the
5 owner of private wetlands in order to construct a certain pier under certain
6 circumstances; and generally relating to wetlands and waterways permits and the
7 construction of piers.

8 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,

12 (2014 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

15 Section 16–105.1
16 Annotated Code of Maryland
17 (2014 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

18 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
19 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

20 Article – Environment

21 16–101.

22 (a) In this title the following words have the meanings indicated.

23 (k) (1) “Pier” means any pier, wharf, dock, walkway, bulkhead, breakwater,
24 piles, or other similar structure.

1 (2) “Pier” does not include any structure on pilings or stilts that was
2 originally constructed beyond the landward boundaries of State or private wetlands.

3 (l) (1) “Private wetlands” means any land not considered “State wetland”
4 bordering on or lying beneath tidal waters, which is subject to regular or periodic tidal
5 action and supports aquatic growth.

6 (2) “Private wetlands” includes wetlands, transferred by the State by a
7 valid grant, lease, patent, or grant confirmed by Article 5 of the Maryland Declaration of
8 Rights, to the extent of the interest transferred.

9 16–105.1.

10 (A) SUBJECT TO SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION AND NOTWITHSTANDING
11 ANY OTHER PROVISION OF THIS TITLE, A PERSON MAY NOT BE REQUIRED TO OBTAIN
12 PERMISSION FROM THE OWNER OF PRIVATE WETLANDS IN ORDER TO CONSTRUCT A
13 PIER THAT IS:

14 (1) WITHIN THE PRIVATE WETLANDS;

15 (2) CONNECTED TO A LIGHTHOUSE; AND

16 (3) NOT CONNECTED TO THE SHORE.

17 (B) A PERSON UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION SHALL OBTAIN ALL
18 NECESSARY APPROVALS FROM THE BOARD AND THE DEPARTMENT PRIOR TO
19 CONSTRUCTION OF THE PIER.

20 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
21 October 1, 2026.
