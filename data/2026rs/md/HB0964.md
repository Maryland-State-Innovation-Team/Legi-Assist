A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Secure the Vote Act of 2026

3 FOR the purpose of repealing the requirements that the Baltimore City centralized booking
4 facility provide a ballot drop box to eligible voters and the State Board of Elections
5 provide a ballot drop box to the Baltimore City centralized booking facility; repealing
6 the requirement that each local board of elections designate locations in the county
7 at which ballot drop boxes will be placed and submit the proposed locations to the
8 State Administrator of Elections; repealing security requirements and procedures
9 relating to ballot drop boxes; repealing provisions of law governing permanent
10 absentee ballot status; repealing the authority to conduct special elections by mail;
11 establishing requirements related to proof of citizenship of individuals registering to
12 vote, voter identification, absentee ballot signature verification, audits of the
13 statewide voter registration list, and the conduct of municipal elections; restricting
14 absentee voting to individuals who are unable to vote in person due to certain
15 circumstances; establishing prohibitions related to the return of absentee ballots, the
16 counting of absentee ballots, and municipal voter registration of individuals who are
17 not citizens of the United States; and generally relating to the conduct of elections.
