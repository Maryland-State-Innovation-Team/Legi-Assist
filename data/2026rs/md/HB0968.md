A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Corporations and Associations – Maryland Securities Act – Filing Fees

3 FOR the purpose of increasing the fee that a person required to submit a filing in
4 accordance with a certain exemption is required to pay; increasing the fee that a
5 person required to submit a notice of offer or sale of certain federal covered securities
6 is required to pay; and generally relating to filing fees required under the Maryland
7 Securities Act.

8 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
9 Article – Corporations and Associations
10 Section 11–506
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2025 Replacement Volume)

13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

15 Article – Corporations and Associations

16 11–506.

17 (a) Except as provided in § 11–510.1 of this subtitle, a person filing an application
18 to register securities shall pay a fee of 0.1 percent of the maximum aggregate offering price
19 at which the securities are to be offered in this State, but the fee may not be less than $500
20 or more than $1,500.

21 (b) (1) Except as provided in paragraph (2) of this subsection, a person
22 required to submit a filing in accordance with an exemption granted under this title shall
23 pay a fee of $400 for each filing.
