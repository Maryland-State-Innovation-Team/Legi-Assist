A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Safety – Maryland Police Training and Standards Commission –
3 Prohibition Against Certain Affiliation or Support by Police Officers

4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Police Training and Standards Commission to
5 prohibit police officers from affiliating with, supporting, or advocating for White
6 supremacist groups, ideologies, or causes or participating in or actively promoting a
7 certain extremist group; and generally relating to the Maryland Police Training and
8 Standards Commission.

1 (b) “Commission” means the Maryland Police Training and Standards

4 (P) (1) (I) IN THIS SUBSECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE
5 MEANINGS INDICATED.

6 (II) “BIPOC” MEANS BLACK PEOPLE, INDIGENOUS PEOPLE,
7 AND PEOPLE OF COLOR.

8 (III) “WHITE SUPREMACIST GROUPS, CAUSES, OR IDEOLOGIES”
9 MEANS GROUPS, CAUSES, OR IDEOLOGIES THAT:

10 1. PROMOTE WHITE SUPREMACY AND THE IDEA THAT
11 WHITE PEOPLE ARE SUPERIOR TO BIPOC;

12 2. PROMOTE RELIGIOUS AND RACIAL BIGOTRY;

13 3. SEEK TO EXACERBATE RACIAL AND ETHNIC TENSIONS
14 BETWEEN BIPOC AND NON–BIPOC; OR

15 4. ENGAGE IN PATENTLY HATEFUL AND INFLAMMATORY
16 SPEECH, INTIMIDATION, AND VIOLENCE AGAINST BIPOC AS A MEANS OF
17 PROMOTING WHITE SUPREMACY.

18 (2) THE COMMISSION SHALL PROHIBIT POLICE OFFICERS FROM:

19 (I) AFFILIATING WITH, SUPPORTING, OR ADVOCATING FOR
20 WHITE SUPREMACIST GROUPS, CAUSES, OR IDEOLOGIES; OR

21 (II) PARTICIPATING IN OR ACTIVELY PROMOTING AN
22 INTERNATIONAL OR DOMESTIC EXTREMIST GROUP THAT THE FEDERAL BUREAU OF
23 INVESTIGATION HAS DETERMINED SUPPORTS OR ENCOURAGES ILLEGAL VIOLENT
24 CONDUCT.

25 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
26 October 1, 2026.
