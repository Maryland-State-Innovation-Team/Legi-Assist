A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Motor Vehicles – Speed Monitoring Systems – Safety Corridors
3 (Vulnerable Road User Protection Act of 2026)

4 FOR the purpose of authorizing the State Highway Administration to implement a program
5 of safety corridor speed monitoring systems for use in areas determined to be of high
6 risk to vulnerable road users; authorizing a local jurisdiction to use speed monitoring
7 systems in safety corridors under certain circumstances; and generally relating to
8 the use of speed monitoring systems in safety corridors.
