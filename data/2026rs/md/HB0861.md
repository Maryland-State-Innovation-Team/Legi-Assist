A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Department of Information Technology – Statewide Information Technology
3 Master Plan – Reporting

4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of Information Technology to post the
5 Statewide Information Technology Master Plan on its website on or before a certain
6 date; requiring the Secretary of Information Technology to notify the Governor and
7 certain committees of the General Assembly if the plan is delayed; and generally
8 relating to the Statewide Information Technology Master Plan.

9 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
10 Article – State Finance and Procurement
11 Section 3.5–304
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

14 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

16 Article – State Finance and Procurement

17 3.5–304.

18 (A) The Secretary shall develop a statewide information technology master plan.

19 (B) (1) THE PLAN DEVELOPED UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS SECTION
20 SHALL BE POSTED PUBLICLY ON THE DEPARTMENT’S WEBSITE WITHIN 6 MONTHS
21 AFTER THE DATE THE GOVERNOR IS INAUGURATED IN THE GOVERNOR’S FIRST
22 TERM.
