A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Safety – Distribution of Literature to Purchasers of Firearms and
3 Ammunition

4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Maryland Department of Health to prepare certain
5 literature relating to firearms and ammunition and to distribute the literature to all
6 county health departments in the State; requiring each county health department to
7 distribute certain literature to all establishments that sell firearms or ammunition
8 within the county; requiring establishments that sell firearms or ammunition in the
9 State to make certain literature visible and available and to distribute the literature
10 to purchasers of firearms or ammunition; requiring a county health department to
11 authorize a certain representative who may issue citations to owners of
12 establishments who violate this Act; and generally relating to the distribution of
13 literature to purchasers of firearms and ammunition.

14 BY adding to
15 Article – Public Safety
16 Section 5–901 and 5–902 to be under the new subtitle “Subtitle 9. Distribution of
17 Literature to Purchasers of Firearms and Ammunition”

14 (II) DISTRIBUTE THE LITERATURE TO ALL COUNTY HEALTH
15 DEPARTMENTS IN THE STATE.

16 (2) EACH COUNTY HEALTH DEPARTMENT SHALL DISTRIBUTE THE
17 LITERATURE RECEIVED UNDER PARAGRAPH (1) OF THIS SUBSECTION TO ALL
18 ESTABLISHMENTS THAT SELL FIREARMS OR AMMUNITION WITHIN THE COUNTY.

19 (B) AN ESTABLISHMENT THAT RECEIVES LITERATURE UNDER SUBSECTION
20 (A)(2) OF THIS SECTION SHALL:

21 (1) MAKE THE LITERATURE VISIBLE AND AVAILABLE AT THE POINT
22 OF SALE FOR ANY FIREARM OR AMMUNITION PURCHASE; AND

23 (2) DISTRIBUTE THE LITERATURE TO EACH PERSON WHO PURCHASES
24 A FIREARM OR AMMUNITION.
