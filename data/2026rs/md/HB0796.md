A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Recycling – Prohibition on the Chemical Conversion of Plastic

3 FOR the purpose of altering the definition of recycling to exclude certain chemical
4 conversion processes; prohibiting a person from building in the State a facility that
5 converts plastic to fuel or feedstock through certain chemical conversion processes;
6 and generally relating to recycling and the chemical conversion of plastic.

3 (q) (1) “Recycling” means any process in which recyclable materials are
4 collected, separated, or processed and returned to the marketplace in the form of raw
5 materials or products.

6 (2) (I) “RECYCLING” DOES NOT INCLUDE ANY OF THE FOLLOWING
7 CHEMICAL CONVERSION PROCESSES:

8 1. PYROLYSIS;

9 2. HYDROPYROLYSIS;

10 3. METHANOLYSIS;

11 4. GASIFICATION; OR

12 5. ENZYMATIC BREAKDOWN.

13 (II) “RECYCLING” DOES NOT INCLUDE ANY SIMILAR CHEMICAL
14 CONVERSION PROCESSES, AS DETERMINED BY THE DEPARTMENT.
