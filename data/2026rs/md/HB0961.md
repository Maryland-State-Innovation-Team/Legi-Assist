A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Schools and Youth Sports Programs – Concussion Protocol – Alterations

3 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Department of Education to include athletic
4 officials in certain policies and in a certain program to provide awareness on a certain
5 concussion protocol for certain public school and youth sports programs; establishing
6 that certain individuals may remove a student from play and prevent a student from
7 returning to play under certain circumstances; authorizing a person to bring a
8 certain action for declaratory relief to enforce certain provisions of law; and generally
9 relating to a concussion protocol for public school and youth sports programs.

4 (c) (1) A student who is suspected of sustaining a concussion or other head
