By: Delegates Buckel, Adams, Arentz, Chisholm, Ghrist, Griffith, Hartman,

Introduced and read first time: February 5, 2026
Assigned to: Environment and Transportation

A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Natural Gas – Connection and Line Extension – Discounts and Payment Plans

3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting the Public Service Commission from adopting or enforcing
4 a regulation or order that prohibits a public service company from offering a discount
5 or payment plan for the connection or extension of a natural gas line to a customer’s
6 property; and generally relating to natural gas distribution.

7 BY adding to
8 Article – Public Utilities
9 Section 7–321
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

14 Article – Public Utilities

15 7–321.

16 THE COMMISSION MAY NOT ADOPT OR ENFORCE ANY REGULATION OR ORDER
17 THAT PROHIBITS A PUBLIC SERVICE COMPANY FROM OFFERING A DISCOUNT OR
18 PAYMENT PLAN FOR THE CONNECTION OR EXTENSION OF A NATURAL GAS LINE TO A
19 CUSTOMER’S PROPERTY.

20 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
21 October 1, 2026.
