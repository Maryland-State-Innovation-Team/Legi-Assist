10 (c) [The minimum annual salary for the register in Baltimore City is $12,000.

11 (d)] (1) The salaries of the registers shall be paid semimonthly from the fees
12 and receipts of the office, after deducting the expenses of the office.

13 (2) Expenses include salaries of deputies and clerks, books, stationery,
14 office supplies, and other necessary and customary expenses of doing business.

15 [(e)] (D) (1) (i) If the fees and receipts of the office are insufficient in any
16 fiscal year to pay all or a part of the expenses of the office and authorized salary of a
17 register, the deficiency shall be funded from the taxes remitted to the Comptroller by the
18 register during that fiscal year.

19 (ii) Written authority for the transfer of funds shall be first obtained
20 from the Comptroller.

21 (2) In the event that tax collections for the fiscal year are insufficient, the
22 Comptroller shall make up the deficit from excess fees remitted from all other registers.

23 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, in accordance with Article
24 III, § 35 of the Maryland Constitution, this Act may not be construed to extend or apply to
25 the salary or compensation of a clerk of a circuit court or a register of wills while serving in
26 a term of office beginning before the effective date of this Act, but the provisions of this Act
27 concerning the salary or compensation of a clerk of the circuit court or a register of wills
28 shall take effect at the beginning of the next following term of office. This limitation does
29 not apply to an individual appointed or elected after the effective date of this Act to fill out
30 an unexpired term.

31 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
32 October 1, 2026.
