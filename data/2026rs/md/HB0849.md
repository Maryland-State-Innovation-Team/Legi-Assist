A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Child Care Scholarship Program – Freeze in Enrollment – Exceptions

3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a freeze in enrollment in the Child Care Scholarship
4 Program from including certain individuals enrolled in certain governmental
5 support programs, children with siblings enrolled in the Program, individuals with
6 certain employment issues, and individuals employed by a child care provider; and
7 generally relating to exceptions to a freeze in enrollment in the Child Care
8 Scholarship Program.

9 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
10 Article – Education
11 Section 9.5–111
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

15 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

18 (a) (1) In this section the following words have the meanings indicated.

19 (2) “Analysis” means the market rate survey or an alternative method
20 allowable under federal law.

21 (3) “Program” means the Child Care Scholarship Program.
