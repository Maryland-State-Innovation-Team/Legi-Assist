A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Natural Resources – Recreational Striped Bass Catch – Reporting

3 FOR the purpose of requiring every person who catches striped bass under a Chesapeake
4 Bay and coastal sport fishing license or registration to report the catch to the
5 Department of Natural Resources; requiring the Department to adopt regulations to
6 carry out this Act, including regulations authorizing certain reporting methods,
7 authorizing a person to report the catch through a mobile application to the
8 maximum extent feasible, and establishing a reporting method that is usable by
9 persons who do not have Internet access; and generally relating to reporting
10 requirements for the recreational catching of striped bass.

1 (a) (1) Except as provided in subsections (c) and (d) of this section and § 4–217
2 of this title, a person may not fish for finfish in the Chesapeake Bay or in its tributaries up
3 to tidal boundaries or in State waters of the Atlantic Ocean and the Atlantic coastal bays
4 and their tributaries without first obtaining a Chesapeake Bay and coastal sport fishing
5 license or registration issued under subsection (d)(3) of this section and possessing the
6 license or registration.

7 (f) Every person licensed under this section shall submit any report the
8 Department requires in accordance with Department regulations.

9 (G) (1) EVERY PERSON WHO CATCHES STRIPED BASS UNDER A
10 CHESAPEAKE BAY AND COASTAL SPORT FISHING LICENSE OR REGISTRATION SHALL
11 REPORT THE CATCH TO THE DEPARTMENT.

12 (2) (I) THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ADOPT REGULATIONS TO CARRY
13 OUT THIS SUBSECTION.

14 (II) REGULATIONS ADOPTED UNDER THIS PARAGRAPH SHALL:

15 1. AUTHORIZE REPORTING METHODS THAT THE
16 DEPARTMENT DETERMINES TO BE FEASIBLE;

17 2. TO THE MAXIMUM EXTENT FEASIBLE, AUTHORIZE A
18 PERSON TO REPORT THE CATCH THROUGH A MOBILE APPLICATION; AND

19 3. ESTABLISH A REPORTING METHOD THAT IS USABLE
20 BY PERSONS WHO DO NOT HAVE INTERNET ACCESS.

21 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
22 January 1, 2027.
