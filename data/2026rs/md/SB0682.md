A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Anne Arundel County – Community Sewerage Systems – Homeowners
3 Association Control

4 FOR the purpose of authorizing a homeowners association in Anne Arundel County to
5 manage, operate, and maintain a shared facility or community sewerage system as
6 a controlling authority if certain conditions are met; requiring the Maryland
7 Environmental Service to take charge of and operate the shared facility or
8 community sewerage system under certain circumstances; and generally relating to
9 shared facilities and community sewerage systems in Anne Arundel County.
