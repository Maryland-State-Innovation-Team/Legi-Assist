1 (2) “CLEAN AND SANITIZE” MEANS TO REMOVE RESIDUE,
2 CONTAMINANTS, ODORS, AND HARMFUL MICROORGANISMS FROM ALL CONTAINER
3 SURFACES.

4 (3) “PROFESSIONAL CLEANING SERVICE” MEANS A LICENSED ENTITY
5 CAPABLE OF:

6 (I) CLEANING AND SANITIZING SOLID WASTE CONTAINERS;
7 AND

8 (II) AS NECESSARY, TESTING SOLID WASTE CONTAINERS FOR
9 HARMFUL BACTERIA AND PATHOGENS USING METHODS APPROVED BY THE
10 DEPARTMENT.

11 (4) “SOLID WASTE CONTAINER” MEANS A TRASH BIN, DUMPSTER, OR
12 SIMILAR RECEPTACLE USED FOR THE STORAGE OF MUNICIPAL SOLID WASTE.

13 (B) THIS SECTION DOES NOT APPLY TO A SOLID WASTE CONTAINER USED
14 FOR THE STORAGE OF MUNICIPAL SOLID WASTE FROM A SINGLE–FAMILY HOME.

15 (C) (1) THE OWNER OF A SOLID WASTE CONTAINER SHALL CONTRACT
16 WITH A PROFESSIONAL CLEANING SERVICE TO CLEAN AND SANITIZE THE SOLID
17 WASTE CONTAINER AT LEAST ONCE PER MONTH.

18 (2) A PROFESSIONAL CLEANING SERVICE CONTRACTED UNDER THIS
19 SUBSECTION SHALL:

20 (I) IMPLEMENT CLEANING AND SANITIZATION PROTOCOLS
21 THAT INCLUDE:

22 1. THE USE OF DISINFECTANTS APPROVED BY THE
23 DEPARTMENT;

24 2. HIGH–PRESSURE WASHING; AND

25 3. THE REMOVAL OF ALL ODORS AND VISIBLE
26 RESIDUES; AND

27 (II) MAINTAIN DOCUMENTATION OF EACH CLEANING AND
28 SANITIZATION SERVICE RENDERED TO THE OWNER OF A SOLID WASTE CONTAINER
29 FOR AT LEAST 12 MONTHS AFTER THE SERVICE DATE.
