A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Schools – School–Based Mental Health Services – Full–Time Therapist

3 FOR the purpose of requiring each public school to employ a full–time therapist that
4 provides school–based mental health services to students during the instructional
5 day; requiring the full–time therapist to be licensed and certified by the State Board
6 of Professional Counselors and Therapists; and generally relating to mental health
7 services in public schools.

18 provide:

19 (1) Adequate school health services;

21 activity in maintaining good health; and
