A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Prince George’s County Special Education Service Delivery, Transparency, and
3 Accountability Act

4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Prince George’s County Board of Education and County
5 Superintendent, beginning in a certain school year, to include certain funds in their
6 annual budget to certain individuals hired as one–to–one student aides; requiring
7 the County Superintendent, or the County Superintendent’s designee, to provide
8 certain quarterly updates to the county board and the State Department of
9 Education; requiring the County Superintendent to develop and offer certain
10 professional development training for employees who provide special education
11 services; requiring the State Department of Education to hire a third–party auditor
12 to conduct a certain audit of the Department of Special Education in Prince George’s
13 County Public Schools for certain fiscal years; and generally relating to special
14 education and Prince George’s County Public Schools.

30 (iv) the duration of unfilled assignments;
