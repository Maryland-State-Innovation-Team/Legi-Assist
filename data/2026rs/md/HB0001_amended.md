A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Investor–Owned Electric, Gas, and Gas and Electric Companies – Cost Recovery
3 – Limitations

4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting certain public service companies from recovering
5 through rates any costs associated with a supervisor’s annual compensation once the
6 compensation exceeds a certain amount; requiring the boards of directors of certain
7 public service companies to adopt certain policies to place reasonable cost limitations
8 on certain types of expenditures; requiring the Public Service Commission to publish
9 certain guidance; requiring each public service company to send a copy of the policy
10 adopted under this Act to the Commission at certain times; and generally relating to
11 cost recovery by public service companies.
