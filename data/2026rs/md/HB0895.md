A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Food Retailers – Dynamic Pricing, Surveillance Data, and Collective Bargaining
3 Agreements
4 (Protection From Predatory Pricing Act)

5 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a food retailer from engaging in the practice of dynamic
6 pricing or using consumer surveillance data to set a price for consumer goods or
7 services; prohibiting a food retailer from using protected class data to offer,
8 advertise, or sell a consumer good or service under certain circumstances; prohibiting
9 a food retailer from diminishing or impairing any right or benefit guaranteed to
10 employees of the food retailer under an existing collective bargaining agreement or
11 memorandum of understanding under certain circumstances; making a certain
12 violation of this Act an unfair, abusive, or deceptive trade practice that is subject to
13 enforcement and penalties under the Maryland Consumer Protection Act; and
14 generally relating to the regulation of food retailers.

14 Article – Commercial Law

15 13–321.

16 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
17 INDICATED.

18 (2) “ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE” HAS THE MEANING STATED IN §
19 3.5–801 OF THE STATE FINANCE AND PROCUREMENT ARTICLE.

20 (3) (I) “DYNAMIC PRICING” MEANS THE PRACTICE OF VARYING
21 THE PRICES OF CONSUMER GOODS OR SERVICES WITHIN A BUSINESS DAY BASED ON
22 DEMAND OR OTHER FACTORS, INCLUDING THROUGH THE USE OF ARTIFICIAL
23 INTELLIGENCE OR MODELS THAT RETRAIN OR RECALIBRATE BASED ON RECEIVED
24 INFORMATION IN NEAR REAL–TIME.

25 (II) “DYNAMIC PRICING” DOES NOT INCLUDE THE USE OF
26 PROMOTIONAL PRICING OFFERS, LOYALTY PROGRAM BENEFITS, OR OTHER
27 TEMPORARY DISCOUNTS OR CHANGES TO PRICING RELATED TO RETENTION OF
28 EXISTING CUSTOMERS.

29 (4) “FOOD RETAILER” MEANS A MERCHANT THAT OPERATES A
30 BUSINESS ESTABLISHMENT THAT:

31 (I) HAS A MINIMUM OF 15,000 SQUARE FEET; AND

32 (II) SELLS FOOD THAT IS EXEMPT FROM THE SALES AND USE
33 TAX IN ACCORDANCE WITH § 11–206(C) OF THE TAX – GENERAL ARTICLE.

1 (5) “SURVEILLANCE DATA” MEANS CONSUMER INFORMATION
