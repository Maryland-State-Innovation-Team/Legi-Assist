A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Electric Companies – Environmental Surcharges or Fees – Prohibition on
3 Collection

4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting an electric company from collecting certain environmental
5 surcharges or fees under certain circumstances; requiring the Public Service
6 Commission to make a certain calculation each year related to the change in
7 residential electric bills; and generally relating to the collection of environmental
8 surcharges or fees by electric companies.
