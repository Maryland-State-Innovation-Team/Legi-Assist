A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Family Law – Child Abuse and Neglect Investigations
3 (“Know Before They Knock” Family Right to Notice Act)

4 FOR the purpose of requiring a certain on–site interview following a report of suspected
5 child abuse to be with the child’s parent or guardian rather than the child’s
6 caretaker; requiring a local department of social services or a law enforcement
7 agency to provide a certain notice to a parent or legal guardian of a child at a certain
8 time during an investigation of suspected child abuse or neglect; and generally
9 relating to child abuse and neglect.

10 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
11 Article – Family Law
12 Section 5–706
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
