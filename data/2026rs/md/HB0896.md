A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Military Department – National Guard State Active Duty Mobilization Fund

3 FOR the purpose of establishing the National Guard State Active Duty Mobilization Fund
4 as a special, nonlapsing fund in the Military Department to provide funds for State
5 active duty mobilization of the National Guard; and generally relating to the
6 National Guard State Active Duty Mobilization Fund.

7 BY adding to
8 Article – Public Safety
9 Section 13–220
10 Annotated Code of Maryland
11 (2022 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

12 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
13 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

14 Article – Public Safety

15 13–220.

16 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “FUND” MEANS THE NATIONAL GUARD STATE
17 ACTIVE DUTY MOBILIZATION FUND.

18 (B) THERE IS A NATIONAL GUARD STATE ACTIVE DUTY MOBILIZATION
19 FUND IN THE OFFICE OF THE ADJUTANT GENERAL WITHIN THE DEPARTMENT.

20 (C) THE PURPOSE OF THE FUND IS TO PAY FOR PERSONNEL AND
21 EQUIPMENT FOR STATE ACTIVE DUTY MOBILIZATION OF THE NATIONAL GUARD, AS
22 ORDERED BY THE GOVERNOR.

1 (D) THE ADJUTANT GENERAL SHALL ADMINISTER THE FUND.

2 (E) (1) THE FUND IS A SPECIAL, NONLAPSING FUND THAT IS NOT
3 SUBJECT TO § 7–302 OF THE STATE FINANCE AND PROCUREMENT ARTICLE.

4 (2) THE STATE TREASURER SHALL HOLD THE FUND SEPARATELY,
5 AND THE COMPTROLLER SHALL ACCOUNT FOR THE FUND.

6 (F) THE FUND CONSISTS OF:

7 (1) MONEY APPROPRIATED IN THE STATE BUDGET TO THE FUND; AND

8 (2) ANY OTHER MONEY FROM ANY OTHER SOURCE ACCEPTED FOR
9 THE BENEFIT OF THE FUND.

10 (G) THE FUND MAY BE USED ONLY FOR THE EXPENSES OF AND TO SUPPORT
11 THE OPERATIONS OF THE NATIONAL GUARD IN STATE ACTIVE DUTY STATUS.

12 (H) THE STATE TREASURER SHALL INVEST THE MONEY OF THE FUND IN
13 THE SAME MANNER AS OTHER STATE MONEY MAY BE INVESTED.

14 (I) ANY INVESTMENT EARNINGS OF THE FUND SHALL BE CREDITED TO THE
15 GENERAL FUND OF THE STATE.

16 (J) EXPENDITURES FROM THE FUND MAY BE MADE ONLY IN ACCORDANCE
17 WITH THE STATE BUDGET.

18 (K) MONEY EXPENDED FROM THE FUND FOR STATE ACTIVE DUTY
19 MOBILIZATION OF THE NATIONAL GUARD IS SUPPLEMENTAL TO AND IS NOT
20 INTENDED TO TAKE THE PLACE OF FUNDING THAT OTHERWISE WOULD BE
21 APPROPRIATED FOR STATE ACTIVE DUTY MOBILIZATION OF THE NATIONAL GUARD.

22 (L) MONEY EXPENDED FROM THE FUND SHALL BE REPLENISHED IN A
23 TIMELY MANNER TO MAINTAIN THE OPERATIONAL EFFECTIVENESS OF FUTURE
24 STATE ACTIVE DUTY MOBILIZATIONS OF THE NATIONAL GUARD.

25 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
26 1, 2026.
