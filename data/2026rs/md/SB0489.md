A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Health Occupations – Physicians Trained in International Medical Schools

3 FOR the purpose of establishing that certain applicants for a physician license are exempt
4 from certain educational requirements if the applicant submits certain evidence of
5 international postgraduate medical education training accredited by a certain entity;
6 authorizing certain licensed physicians trained in international medical schools to
7 practice medicine during their initial license term only under supervision; and
8 generally relating to physicians trained in international medical schools.
