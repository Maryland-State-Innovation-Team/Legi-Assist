A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Primary and Secondary Education – Funding Accuracy and Full–Time
3 Equivalent Enrollment Count – Alterations and Report
4 (Education Funding Accuracy Act)

5 FOR the purpose of altering the definition of “full–time equivalent enrollment” in the
6 calculation for State education aid to include the average number of students
7 enrolled in kindergarten through grade 12 on certain days of the prior school year;
8 requiring the State Department of Education to publish online and submit a certain
9 report to certain standing committees of the General Assembly; and generally
10 relating to the calculation of State aid for primary and secondary education.

2 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
3 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

4 Article – Education

5 5–201.

6 (a) In this subtitle, except as otherwise provided, the following words have the
7 meanings indicated.

8 (e) “Enrollment count” means the greater of:

9 (1) The full–time equivalent enrollment; or

10 (2) The 3–year moving average enrollment.

11 (f) “Foundation program” means the product of the annual per pupil foundation
12 amount and a county’s enrollment count.

13 (g) “Full–time equivalent enrollment” means the sum of:

14 (1) The AVERAGE number of students enrolled in kindergarten through
15 grade 12 or their equivalent in regular day school programs on September 30 AND MAY 31
16 of the prior school year;

17 (2) The number of full–time equivalent students, as determined by a
18 regulation of the Department, enrolled in evening high school programs during the prior
19 school year; and

20 (3) The number of P–TECH students, as provided in § 7–1804(b) of this
21 article.

22 (t) (1) Except as provided in paragraph (2) of this subsection, “3–year moving
23 average enrollment” means the average of the full–time equivalent enrollment in the 3
24 prior school years.

25 (2) If the 3 prior school years includes the 2020–2021 school year, “3–year
26 moving average enrollment” means:

27 (i) The sum of the full–time equivalent enrollment for the 4 prior
28 school years minus the 2020–2021 school year full–time equivalent enrollment; divided by

29 (ii) Three.
