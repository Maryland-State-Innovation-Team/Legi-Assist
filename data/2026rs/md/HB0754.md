1 (b) The Board of License Commissioners may issue the license for the use of a golf
2 course or organization that:

3 (1) is open to the public;

4 (2) is operated for profit;

5 (3) owns real estate in the county; and

6 (4) has a golf course with a minimum of 18 holes.

7 (c) (1) The license holder may sell beer, wine, and liquor for consumption on
8 the land and in the buildings, including the clubhouse, used for golfing purposes.

9 (2) A patron need not be seated to be served.

10 (d) A holder of the license may sell beer, wine, and liquor:

11 (1) on Monday through Saturday, from [10 a.m.] 7 A.M. to 2 a.m. the
12 following day; and

13 (2) on Sunday, from [10 a.m.] 7 A.M. to midnight.

14 (e) The annual license fee is $2,200.

15 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
16 1, 2026.
