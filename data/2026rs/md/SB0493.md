A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Nursing Facilities – Involuntary Discharge or Transfer

3 FOR the purpose of prohibiting certain facilities from involuntarily transferring or
4 discharging a resident to certain housing or before receiving certain confirmations;
5 altering the information required to be included in a certain notice or updated notice;
6 requiring that a certain notice or updated notice be provided to certain residents at
7 least a certain period of time before an involuntary discharge or transfer; requiring
8 that a post discharge plan contain certain information; and generally relating to
9 nursing facilities and involuntary discharges and transfers.

10 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
11 Article – Health – General
12 Section 19–345 and 19–345.1
