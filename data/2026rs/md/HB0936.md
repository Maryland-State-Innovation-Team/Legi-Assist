A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Montgomery County Public Schools – Local Food Procurement

3 MC 7–26

4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Montgomery County public school system to identify and
5 publish certain data on food procured during a certain school year from certified local
6 farms and certified Chesapeake invasive species providers; requiring the
7 Montgomery County public school system to set certain targets for certain school
8 years; requiring the Montgomery County public school system, in coordination with
9 the Office of Food Systems Resilience and public input, to establish a certain plan;
10 and generally relating to food procured for Montgomery County Public Schools from
11 certified local farms and Chesapeake invasive species providers.
