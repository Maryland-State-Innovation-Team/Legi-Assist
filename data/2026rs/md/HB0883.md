A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Consumer Protection – Artificial Intelligence – Behavioral Health Care
3 Prohibitions

4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting a certain developer from making or knowingly causing
5 artificial intelligence to make certain representations or statements or to provide
6 certain services or experiences relating to behavioral health care, subject to a certain
7 civil penalty; requiring certain artificial intelligence sold, leased, given, or otherwise
8 provided to consumers in the State to include certain notice or certain protocols;
9 authorizing the Attorney General to investigate and bring an action for an alleged
10 violation of this Act and requiring revenues from civil penalties collected to be
11 distributed to a certain fund; and generally relating to artificial intelligence and
12 behavioral health care.

13 BY adding to
14 Article – Commercial Law
15 Section 14–5101 and 14–5102 to be under the new subtitle “Subtitle 51. Artificial
16 Intelligence”

1 (A) IN THIS SUBTITLE THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS

3 (B) “ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE” HAS THE MEANING STATED IN § 3.5–801
4 OF THE STATE FINANCE AND PROCUREMENT ARTICLE.

5 (C) “BEHAVIORAL HEALTH” HAS THE MEANING STATED IN § 7.5–101 OF THE
6 HEALTH – GENERAL ARTICLE.

7 (D) “BEHAVIORAL HEALTH CARE” HAS THE MEANING STATED IN § 7.5–101
8 OF THE HEALTH – GENERAL ARTICLE.

9 (E) (1) “BEHAVIORAL HEALTH CARE PROVIDER” MEANS AN INDIVIDUAL
10 WHO IS LICENSED OR OTHERWISE AUTHORIZED TO PROVIDE BEHAVIORAL HEALTH
11 CARE SERVICES UNDER THE HEALTH OCCUPATIONS ARTICLE.

12 (2) “BEHAVIORAL HEALTH CARE PROVIDER” INCLUDES:

13 (I) A COUNSELOR;

14 (II) A PSYCHIATRIST;

15 (III) A PSYCHOLOGIST;

16 (IV) A SOCIAL WORKER; AND

17 (V) A THERAPIST.

18 (F) “DEVELOPER” MEANS A PERSON THAT OFFERS FOR SALE, SELLS,
19 LEASES, GIVES, OR OTHERWISE PROVIDES ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE TO A
20 CONSUMER IN THE STATE.

21 14–5102.

22 (A) A DEVELOPER MAY NOT:

23 (1) MAKE, OR KNOWINGLY CAUSE ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE TO
24 MAKE, ANY REPRESENTATION OR STATEMENT THAT EXPLICITLY OR IMPLICITLY
25 INDICATES THAT THE ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE IS:

26 (I) A BEHAVIORAL HEALTH CARE PROVIDER; OR

27 (II) CAPABLE OF PROVIDING BEHAVIORAL HEALTH CARE; OR

1 (2) OFFER, SELL, LEASE, GIVE, OR OTHERWISE PROVIDE TO
2 CONSUMERS IN THE STATE ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE THAT IS SPECIFICALLY
3 PROGRAMMED OR DESIGNED TO PROVIDE A SERVICE OR AN EXPERIENCE THAT
4 WOULD CONSTITUTE THE PRACTICE OF BEHAVIORAL HEALTH CARE IF PROVIDED BY
5 A BEHAVIORAL HEALTH CARE PROVIDER.

6 (B) ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE SOLD, LEASED, GIVEN, OR OTHERWISE
7 PROVIDED TO A CONSUMER IN THE STATE SHALL INCLUDE:

8 (1) CLEAR AND CONSPICUOUS NOTICE THAT THE CONSUMER IS NOT
9 COMMUNICATING WITH A HUMAN, DISPLAYED AT THE BEGINNING OF EACH USE; AND

10 (2) PROTOCOLS THAT:

11 (I) TAKE REASONABLE STEPS TO DETECT AND ADDRESS
12 SUICIDAL IDEATIONS OR EXPRESSIONS OF SELF–HARM BY A CONSUMER; AND

13 (II) REFER THE CONSUMER TO BEHAVIORAL HEALTH SERVICES
14 OR THE MARYLAND BEHAVIORAL HEALTH CRISIS RESPONSE SYSTEM’S SERVICES
15 WHEN THE ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE DETECTS A CONSUMER’S SUICIDAL
16 IDEATIONS OR EXPRESSIONS OF SELF–HARM.

17 (C) A PERSON THAT VIOLATES SUBSECTION (A) OR (B) OF THIS SECTION IS
18 SUBJECT TO A CIVIL PENALTY NOT EXCEEDING $1,000,000 FOR EACH VIOLATION.

19 (D) THE ATTORNEY GENERAL MAY INVESTIGATE AND BRING AN ACTION
20 AGAINST ANY PERSON FOR AN ALLEGED VIOLATION OF THIS SECTION.

21 (E) THE REVENUES FROM CIVIL PENALTIES ASSESSED FOR A VIOLATION OF
22 THIS SECTION SHALL BE DISTRIBUTED TO THE BEHAVIORAL HEALTH WORKGROUP
23 INVESTMENT FUND ESTABLISHED UNDER § 10–1502 OF THE HEALTH – GENERAL
24 ARTICLE.

25 (F) THIS SECTION MAY NOT BE CONSTRUED TO PROHIBIT:

26 (1) ANY ADVERTISEMENT, STATEMENT, OR REPRESENTATION FOR OR
27 RELATING TO ANY PRODUCT MEANT TO PROVIDE ADVICE AND GUIDANCE RELATING
28 TO BEHAVIORAL HEALTH IF THE PRODUCT DOES NOT CLAIM TO OFFER OR PROVIDE
29 BEHAVIORAL HEALTH CARE; OR

30 (2) A BEHAVIORAL HEALTH CARE PROVIDER FROM OFFERING OR
31 OPERATING ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE PROGRAMMED OR DESIGNED TO BE USED TO
32 SUPPORT ADMINISTRATIVE TASKS.
