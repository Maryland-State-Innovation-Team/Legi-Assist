A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Law Enforcement Officers’ Pension System – Seat Pleasant Police Department

3 FOR the purpose of requiring membership in the Law Enforcement Officers’ Pension
4 System for certain local law enforcement officers of the Seat Pleasant Police
5 Department under certain circumstances; providing that a certain individual is

7 effective date of the Seat Pleasant Police Department’s participation in the Law
8 Enforcement Officers’ Pension System; and generally relating to the Seat Pleasant
9 Police Department’s participation in the Law Enforcement Officers’ Pension System.
