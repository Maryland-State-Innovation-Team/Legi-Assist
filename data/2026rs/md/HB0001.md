5 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS

7 (2) (I) “BONUS” MEANS A FORM OF DIRECT OR INDIRECT
8 PAYMENT, CONSIDERATION, OR COMPENSATION THAT IS PAID OR CONVEYED TO AN
9 EMPLOYEE OF A PUBLIC SERVICE COMPANY IN ADDITION TO THE EMPLOYEE’S BASE
10 PAY.

11 (II) “BONUS” INCLUDES:

12 1. COMPENSATION THAT THE PUBLIC SERVICE
13 COMPANY DOES NOT FORMALLY LABEL AS A BONUS PAYMENT;

14 2. ANY FORM OF INCENTIVE COMPENSATION THE FACT
15 AND AMOUNT OF WHICH IS UNDER THE DISCRETION OF THE PUBLIC SERVICE
16 COMPANY UNTIL A TIME CLOSE TO THE END OF THE PERIOD FOR WHICH THE
17 INCENTIVE PAYMENT IS PAID; AND

18 3. PAYMENTS GIVEN IN ADDITION TO BASE PAY THAT
19 ARE CONTINGENT ON THE OCCURRENCE OF ONE OR MORE EVENTS OR CONDITIONS.

20 (3) (I) “COMPENSATION” MEANS A FORM OF PAYMENT OR
21 CONSIDERATION CONVEYED TO OR FOR THE BENEFIT OF A SUPERVISOR AN

23 SERVICE COMPANY, OR ANY OTHER AFFILIATE OF A PUBLIC SERVICE COMPANY IN
24 CONNECTION WITH THE SUPERVISOR’S EMPLOYEE’S WORK FOR A PUBLIC SERVICE
25 COMPANY.

26 (II) “COMPENSATION” INCLUDES:

27 1. DIRECT AND INDIRECT METHODS OF CONFERRING
28 BENEFITS;

29 2. CASH AND NONCASH BENEFITS;

30 3. SALARY, BONUSES, PERIOD PAYMENTS, AND
