A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Charles County – County Inspector General – Authority Over Charles County
3 Public Schools

4 FOR the purpose of authorizing the governing body of Charles County to enact a local law
5 granting an inspector general established by the county certain authority over
6 Charles County Public Schools; and generally relating to Charles County and
7 Charles County Public Schools.

8 BY adding to
9 Article – Local Government
10 Section 12–906
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2013 Volume and 2025 Supplement)

13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

15 Article – Local Government

16 12–906.

17 (A) THIS SECTION APPLIES ONLY TO CHARLES COUNTY.

18 (B) BEGINNING IN 2027, THE GOVERNING BODY OF CHARLES COUNTY MAY,
19 BY LOCAL LAW, GRANT TO AN INSPECTOR GENERAL ESTABLISHED BY THE COUNTY
20 THE SAME AUTHORITY OVER CHARLES COUNTY PUBLIC SCHOOLS THAT AN
21 INSPECTOR GENERAL WOULD HAVE OVER A DEPARTMENT OF COUNTY
22 GOVERNMENT.
