A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Video Lottery Terminals – Distribution of Local Impact Grants – Allocation for
3 Fair Hill Purses

4 FOR the purpose of altering, for certain fiscal years, the distribution of proceeds from video
5 lottery terminals by requiring a certain portion of local impact grants distributed to
6 Cecil County to be used for purses offered for certain races held at the Fair Hill
7 Natural Resources Management Area; and generally relating to the distribution of
8 proceeds from video lottery terminals and local impact grants.

11 Section 9–1A–31(a)(1)

19 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
20 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

21 Article – State Government

22 9–1A–31.

23 (a) (1) Except as provided in paragraph (8) of this subsection, the local impact
24 grants provided under § 9–1A–27 of this subtitle shall be distributed as provided in this
25 subsection.
