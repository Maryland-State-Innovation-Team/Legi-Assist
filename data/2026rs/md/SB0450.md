A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Carroll County – Office of Permits and Inspections – Denial of Commercial
3 Permit or License for Unpaid Personal Property Taxes

4 FOR the purpose of prohibiting the Carroll County Office of Permits and Inspections from
5 issuing or renewing a commercial license or permit in the county if the applicant has
6 unpaid personal property taxes except under certain circumstances; authorizing the
7 Carroll County Office of Permits and Inspections to issue or renew a commercial
8 license or permit if the applicant submits certain evidence that the applicant has
9 paid any previously unpaid personal property taxes; and generally relating to the
10 issuance or renewal of commercial licenses and permits in Carroll County.
