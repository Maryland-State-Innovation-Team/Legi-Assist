16 (II) ALL TRANSACTIONS INVOLVING REAL PROPERTY HOLDINGS
17 OF SALISBURY NEIGHBORHOOD HOUSING SERVICES, INC., IN THE JURISDICTION
18 GRANTING THE TAX CREDIT.

19 (3) THE GOVERNING BODY OF WICOMICO COUNTY OR THE
20 GOVERNING BODY OF A MUNICIPAL CORPORATION IN WICOMICO COUNTY MAY
21 PROVIDE, BY LAW, FOR:

22 (I) THE AMOUNT, TERMS, SCOPE, AND DURATION OF THE TAX
23 CREDIT UNDER THIS SUBSECTION; AND

24 (II) ANY OTHER PROVISION NECESSARY TO CARRY OUT THIS
25 SUBSECTION.
