A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Brain Health – Guidelines, Grant Fund, and Commemorative Month
3 (Tahjai’s Law)

4 FOR the purpose of requiring the Governor annually to proclaim the month of May to be
5 Brain Health and Chronic Headache Awareness Month; requiring the proclamation
6 to align with national Go Gray in May brain tumor awareness initiatives;
7 establishing the Maryland Brain Health Grant Fund as a special, nonlapsing fund;
8 requiring the Maryland Department of Health, in collaboration with certain entities,
9 to develop guidelines on brain health evaluations; requiring the Department to
10 develop recommendations on brain health screening intervals and insurance parity;
11 and generally relating to brain health.
