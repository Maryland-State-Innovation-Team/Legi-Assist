A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Washington County – Property Tax Credit – Platoon 22, Incorporated

3 FOR the purpose of requiring the governing body of Washington County and of a municipal
4 corporation in Washington County to grant, by law, a certain property tax credit
5 against the county and municipal corporation property tax imposed on certain real
6 property owned by Platoon 22, Incorporated; and generally relating to a property tax
7 credit for real property owned by Platoon 22, Incorporated.

8 BY adding to
9 Article – Tax – Property
10 Section 9–323(h)
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2019 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

15 Article – Tax – Property

16 9–323.

17 (H) THE GOVERNING BODY OF WASHINGTON COUNTY AND OF A MUNICIPAL
18 CORPORATION IN WASHINGTON COUNTY SHALL GRANT, BY LAW, A PROPERTY TAX
19 CREDIT EQUAL TO 100% OF THE COUNTY OR MUNICIPAL CORPORATION PROPERTY
20 TAX IMPOSED ON REAL PROPERTY THAT IS:

21 (1) OWNED BY PLATOON 22, INCORPORATED; AND

22 (2) USED TO PROVIDE HOUSING TO VETERANS.
