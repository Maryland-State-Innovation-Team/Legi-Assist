A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Business Regulation – Abandoned Cemeteries – Acquisition and Disposition

3 FOR the purpose of authorizing a governmental unit to acquire property on which an
4 abandoned cemetery is located under certain circumstances and for a certain
5 purpose; requiring a governmental unit that acquires property under this Act to
6 make a certain effort to contact certain persons for a certain purpose; authorizing a
7 person or organization to bring an action to request the court to transfer property
8 acquired under this Act to the person or organization; and generally relating to
9 abandoned cemeteries.

1 Article – Business Regulation

2 5–805.

3 (a) (1) In this section the following words have the meanings indicated.

4 (2) “Abandoned cemetery” means a cemetery for which:

5 (i) 1. the organization established to engage in the operation of
6 the cemetery has been terminated, has been subject to administrative dissolution by the
7 State, or has otherwise ceased to exist; and

8 2. title has not been conveyed;

9 (ii) there is no property owner listed in the records of the State
10 Department of Assessments and Taxation;

11 (iii) the property has been condemned; or

12 (iv) no person who is legally responsible for the property can be found
13 and legal ownership of the property cannot be determined.

14 5–806.

15 (A) (1) IN THIS SECTION THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
16 INDICATED.

17 (2) “ABANDONED CEMETERY” HAS THE MEANING STATED IN § 5–805
18 OF THIS SUBTITLE.

19 (3) “CULTURAL AFFILIATION” HAS THE MEANING STATED IN § 14–121
20 OF THE REAL PROPERTY ARTICLE.

21 (4) “DESCENDANT COMMUNITY ORGANIZATION” MEANS AN
22 ORGANIZATION WHOSE MEMBERS HAVE ANCESTORS WHO ARE INTERRED IN A
23 PARTICULAR CEMETERY OR WHO HAVE A CULTURAL AFFILIATION OR A RACIAL OR
24 ETHNIC AFFINITY WITH THE DECEASED PERSONS INTERRED IN A PARTICULAR
25 CEMETERY.

26 (B) (1) A GOVERNMENTAL UNIT MAY, BY CONDEMNATION, ACQUIRE
27 PROPERTY ON WHICH AN ABANDONED CEMETERY IS LOCATED WITH THE INTENT TO
28 TRANSFER THE PROPERTY TO:

1 (I) THE DESCENDANTS OF THE DECEASED PERSONS INTERRED
2 IN THE CEMETERY;

3 (II) A DESCENDANT COMMUNITY ORGANIZATION; OR

4 (III) A NONPROFIT ORGANIZATION.

5 (2) A GOVERNMENTAL UNIT USING CONDEMNATION PROCEEDINGS
6 TO ACQUIRE PROPERTY UNDER PARAGRAPH (1) OF THIS SUBSECTION SHALL
7 CONDUCT THE CONDEMNATION IN ACCORDANCE WITH MARYLAND RULE 12–204.

8 (C) (1) A GOVERNMENTAL UNIT THAT ACQUIRES PROPERTY ON WHICH
9 AN ABANDONED CEMETERY IS LOCATED UNDER SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION
10 SHALL MAKE TIMELY AND REASONABLE EFFORTS AFTER ACQUISITION TO CONTACT
11 DESCENDANTS OF THE DECEASED PERSONS INTERRED IN THE ABANDONED
12 CEMETERY IN ORDER TO IDENTIFY DESCENDANTS, A DESCENDANT COMMUNITY
13 ORGANIZATION, OR A NONPROFIT ORGANIZATION TO WHICH THE PROPERTY MAY BE
14 TRANSFERRED.

15 (2) A PERSON OR ORGANIZATION LISTED UNDER SUBSECTION (B)(1)
16 OF THIS SECTION MAY BRING AN ACTION REQUESTING THE COURT TO TRANSFER
17 PROPERTY ACQUIRED BY A GOVERNMENTAL UNIT UNDER THIS SECTION TO THE
18 PERSON OR ORGANIZATION.

19 (D) ANY DESCENDANT, DESCENDANT COMMUNITY ORGANIZATION, OR
20 NONPROFIT ORGANIZATION THAT ACQUIRES PROPERTY UNDER SUBSECTION (B) OF
21 THIS SECTION SHALL MAINTAIN AND PRESERVE THE ABANDONED CEMETERY ON
22 THE PROPERTY.

23 (E) IF A GOVERNMENTAL UNIT THAT ACQUIRES PROPERTY UNDER
24 SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, THE GOVERNMENTAL UNIT:

25 (1) SHALL MAINTAIN AND PRESERVE THE ABANDONED CEMETERY
26 LOCATED ON THE PROPERTY UNTIL THE GOVERNMENTAL UNIT IS ABLE TO
27 TRANSFER THE PROPERTY TO A PERSON DESCRIBED UNDER SUBSECTION (B)(1) OF
28 THIS SECTION; AND

29 (2) IF THE GOVERNMENTAL UNIT IS UNABLE TO TRANSFER THE
30 PROPERTY TO A PERSON DESCRIBED UNDER SUBSECTION (B)(1) OF THIS SECTION
31 IN A REASONABLE PERIOD OF TIME, MAY FACILITATE THE REINTERMENT OF ANY
32 HUMAN REMAINS OR PET REMAINS FROM THE CEMETERY ON THE PROPERTY TO AN
33 APPROPRIATE OPERATIONAL CEMETERY.
