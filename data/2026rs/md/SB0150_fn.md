Notation of Veteran Status - Public Profiles 
 
 
This bill generally expands existing authority related to the indication of veteran status on 
licenses, certificates, or registrations under the purview of the Maryland Department of the 
Environment (MDE), the Department of General Services (DGS), and each unit in the 
Maryland Department of Labor (MD Labor) by authorizing these entities to also include a 
notation indicating veteran status on a person’s public profile. The bill also makes a 
technical correction in statutory provisions that similarly authorize health occupations 
boards to include veteran status on a public profile. 
 
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  The bill can be implemented with existing budgeted resources. No impact 
on revenues. 
  
Local Effect:  The bill is not anticipated to have a material effect on local government 
operations or finances. 
 
Small Business Effect:  None. 
 
 
Analysis 
 
Current Law:  Generally, MDE, DGS, each unit in MD Labor, and each health 
occupations board are authorized to establish a process to include a notation indicating 
veteran status on a license, certificate, or registration under the entity’s purview. Health 
occupations boards are also authorized to include veteran status on public profiles. If such 
a process is established, an application for a license, certificate, or registration must allow
