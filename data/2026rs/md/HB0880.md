A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Maryland Income Tax – Decoupling From Amendments to the Internal Revenue
3 Code – Depreciation and Business Interest Expenses

5 an individual or federal taxable income of a corporation for Maryland income tax

9 Maryland income tax.
