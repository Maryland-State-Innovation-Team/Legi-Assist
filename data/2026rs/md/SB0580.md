A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 State Government – Walk–In Service Requirements

3 FOR the purpose of requiring certain State divisions and programs to provide walk–in
4 service to the public on a first–come, first–served basis at a reasonable frequency
5 and at reasonable locations; and generally relating to walk–in service requirements.

7 Article – State Government
8 Section 10–1703
9 Annotated Code of Maryland
10 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

20 (B) EACH STATE DIVISION OR PROGRAM LISTED UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF
21 THIS SECTION SHALL PROVIDE WALK–IN SERVICE TO THE PUBLIC ON A FIRST–COME,
22 FIRST–SERVED BASIS:

23 (1) AT A REASONABLE FREQUENCY, BUT NOT LESS THAN 1 DAY PER
24 WEEK BETWEEN THE HOURS OF 10:00 A.M. AND 4:00 P.M.; AND

25 (2) SUBJECT TO SUBSECTION (C) OF THIS SECTION, AT REASONABLE
26 LOCATIONS.

27 (C) (1) A DIVISION OR PROGRAM LISTED UNDER SUBSECTION (A) OF THIS
28 SECTION THAT REGULARLY PROVIDES IN–PERSON APPOINTMENTS TO THE PUBLIC
29 SHALL PROVIDE WALK–IN SERVICE IN ACCORDANCE WITH SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS
30 SECTION AT EACH LOCATION WHERE IN–PERSON APPOINTMENTS ARE HELD.
