A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Schools – School Mapping Data – Radius of School Grounds

3 FOR the purpose of requiring the school mapping data produced with funding from the

5 radius surrounding the school grounds; and generally relating to school mapping
6 data.
