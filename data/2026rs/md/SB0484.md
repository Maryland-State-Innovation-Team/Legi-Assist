A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Family Law – Child Abuse and Neglect – Reporting

3 FOR the purpose of altering certain provisions of law relating to the reporting of child abuse
4 and neglect and the reporting of certain information regarding certain children at
5 risk of harm to require reporting to the Department of Human Services instead of to
6 the local department of social services or the appropriate law enforcement agency;
7 requiring the Department to establish a centralized intake system for reports, assess
8 reports for validity, and assign reports to the appropriate local department to
9 investigate; and generally relating to child abuse and neglect.
