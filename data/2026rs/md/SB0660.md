A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Public Safety – Law Enforcement – Response to Immigration Enforcement
3 Actions

4 FOR the purpose of requiring a law enforcement agency to respond to a report of an
5 immigration enforcement activity within the agency’s jurisdiction; requiring a law
6 enforcement officer responding to a report of an immigration enforcement activity to
7 observe and report certain information; requiring that the reports and recordings
8 created in accordance with this Act are subject to the Public Information Act; and
9 generally relating to law enforcement response to immigration enforcement.
