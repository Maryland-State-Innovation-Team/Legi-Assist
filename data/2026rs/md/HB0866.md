A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Office of the Deaf and Hard of Hearing – Case Management Services

3 FOR the purpose of requiring at least a certain number of positions for the Office of the
4 Deaf and Hard of Hearing to hire case managers who specialize in helping deaf,
5 deafblind, and hard of hearing individuals; requiring the Office to provide case
6 management services to deaf, deafblind, and hard of hearing individuals; and
7 generally relating to case management services by the Office of the Deaf and Hard
8 of Hearing.

9 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
10 Article – State Government
11 Section 9–2402 and 9–2403
12 Annotated Code of Maryland
13 (2021 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)
