A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Maryland Medical Assistance Program – Coverage for the Treatment of Obesity

3 FOR the purpose of authorizing, beginning on a certain date, the Maryland Medical
4 Assistance Program to provide comprehensive coverage for the treatment of obesity;
5 requiring the Maryland Department of Health to provide notice to Program
6 recipients if the Department elects to provide the coverage; and generally relating to
7 the Maryland Medical Assistance Program and coverage for the treatment of obesity.

14 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE FOR THE TREATMENT
15 OF OBESITY” INCLUDES COVERAGE FOR INTENSIVE BEHAVIORAL THERAPY,
16 BARIATRIC SURGERY, AND ANY MEDICATION APPROVED BY THE FEDERAL FOOD
17 AND DRUG ADMINISTRATION WITH AN INDICATION FOR CHRONIC WEIGHT
18 MANAGEMENT IN PATIENTS WITH OBESITY.

19 (B) THE PROGRAM MAY PROVIDE COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE FOR THE
20 TREATMENT OF OBESITY.

21 (C) THE PROGRAM MAY UNDERTAKE UTILIZATION MANAGEMENT TO
22 DETERMINE THE MEDICAL NECESSITY AND APPROPRIATENESS FOR TREATMENT OF
23 OBESITY UNDER THIS SECTION IF THE DETERMINATIONS ARE MADE IN THE SAME
24 MANNER AS DETERMINATIONS ARE MADE FOR THE TREATMENT OF ANY OTHER
25 ILLNESS, CONDITION, OR DISORDER COVERED BY THE PROGRAM.

26 (D) IF THE DEPARTMENT ELECTS TO PROVIDE COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE
27 FOR THE TREATMENT OF OBESITY UNDER THIS SECTION, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL
28 PROVIDE NOTICE TO PROGRAM RECIPIENTS.
