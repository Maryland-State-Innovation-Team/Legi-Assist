1 AN ACT concerning

2 Maryland Higher Education Commission – Practical Applications of Real Estate
3 Appraisal (PAREA) Grant Program – Established

6 (A) THERE IS A PRACTICAL APPLICATIONS OF REAL ESTATE APPRAISAL

9 (1) ASSIST IN CLOSING APPRAISAL GAPS THAT OCCUR IN

13 REDLINED NEIGHBORHOODS AND UNDERREPRESENTED COMMUNITIES WITH THE
14 GOAL OF PURSUING PAREA; AND

15 (3) DIVERSIFY THE APPRAISAL FIELD.

16 (C) THE COMMISSION SHALL ADMINISTER THE PROGRAM.
