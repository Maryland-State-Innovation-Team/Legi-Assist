A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Education – Student Athletic Activities – Physical Examinations and
3 Cardiovascular Prescreening

4 FOR the purpose of requiring a certain physical examination used to determine the physical
5 fitness of a student seeking to participate in interscholastic sports to include a
6 certain cardiovascular prescreening; requiring certain health care providers who
7 perform certain physical examinations for students participating in interscholastic
8 sports to include a cardiovascular prescreening as part of the examination; requiring
9 the State Department of Education, in consultation with the Maryland Department
10 of Health to develop certain guidelines; requiring certain nonpublic schools that offer
11 athletic activities for students to develop policies and programs to provide certain
12 information on sudden cardiac arrest to certain persons participating in the
13 nonpublic school’s athletic programs; and generally relating to student athletic
14 activities, physical examinations, and cardiovascular prescreening.

10 7–402.1.

11 (A) IN THIS SECTION, “CARDIOVASCULAR PRESCREENING” MEANS A SET OF
12 TESTS AND EXAMINATIONS DESIGNED TO ASSESS AN INDIVIDUAL’S HEART HEALTH,
13 IDENTIFY RISK FACTORS FOR HEART DISEASE, AND DETECT ANY UNDERLYING
14 CARDIOVASCULAR ABNORMALITIES BEFORE SYMPTOMS APPEAR, INCLUDING:

15 (1) A TARGETED PERSONAL AND FAMILY HISTORY; AND

16 (2) A PHYSICAL EXAMINATION BASED ON NATIONALLY RECOGNIZED,
17 EVIDENCE–BASED GUIDELINES RECOMMENDED BY ORGANIZATIONS FOCUSED ON
18 CARDIOVASCULAR CARE IN PEDIATRIC POPULATIONS.

19 (B) THIS SECTION APPLIES TO PUBLIC SCHOOLS AND NONPUBLIC SCHOOLS
20 THAT HOLD A CERTIFICATE OF APPROVAL ISSUED BY THE STATE BOARD UNDER §
21 2–206 OF THIS ARTICLE THAT REQUIRES STUDENTS TO RECEIVE A PHYSICAL
22 EXAMINATION DETERMINING PHYSICAL FITNESS TO PARTICIPATE IN
23 INTERSCHOLASTIC SPORTS.

24 (C) A PHYSICAL EXAMINATION OF A STUDENT USED TO DETERMINE THE
25 STUDENT’S PHYSICAL FITNESS TO PARTICIPATE IN INTERSCHOLASTIC SPORTS,
26 INCLUDING A TRY–OUT, PRACTICE, OR CONTEST OF A SCHOOL TEAM, SHALL
27 INCLUDE A CARDIOVASCULAR PRESCREENING IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS SECTION
28 BEFORE THE STUDENT PARTICIPATES IN AN INTERSCHOLASTIC SPORT.

29 (D) (1) (I) BEGINNING IN THE 2026–2027 SCHOOL YEAR, A QUALIFIED
30 HEALTH CARE PROVIDER WHO PERFORMS PHYSICAL EXAMINATIONS UNDER § 7–402
31 OF THIS SUBTITLE FOR STUDENTS PARTICIPATING IN INTERSCHOLASTIC SPORTS OR
32 FOR A NONPUBLIC SCHOOL STUDENT, SHALL INCLUDE A CARDIOVASCULAR
33 PRESCREENING AS A PART OF THE EXAMINATION.

8 (c) (1) The Department shall develop policies and implement a program to
9 provide awareness to coaches, school personnel, students, and the parents or guardians of
10 students, in collaboration with the Maryland Department of Health, each county board, the
11 Maryland Public Secondary Schools Athletic Association, the Maryland Athletic Trainers’

13 issues, on:

14 (i) The nature and warning signs of sudden cardiac arrest, including
15 fainting, difficulty breathing, chest pains, dizziness, and abnormal heart rate; and

16 (ii) The risks associated with continuing to play or practice after

18 (2) The program shall include a process to verify that a coach has received
19 information on the program developed under paragraph (1) of this subsection.

20 (3) (i) Before a student enrolled in a public school system in the State
21 may participate in an authorized athletic activity, the county board shall provide a sudden
22 cardiac arrest symptoms and warning signs information sheet to the student and a parent
23 or guardian of the student.

24 (ii) The student and the parent or guardian of the student shall sign
25 a statement acknowledging receipt of the information sheet.

26 (iii) The Department shall create the information sheet and
27 acknowledgment statement required under this paragraph.

28 (4) The Department may use materials available from the Centers for
29 Disease Control and Prevention, Parent Heart Watch, Sudden Arrhythmia Death
30 Syndromes Foundation, or any other appropriate entity to carry out the requirements of
31 this subsection.
