A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Workers’ Compensation – Evaluation of Permanent Impairments – Licensed
3 Social Worker–Clinical

4 FOR the purpose of authorizing a licensed certified social worker–clinical to provide
5 evaluation services for workers’ compensation claims related to permanent
6 impairments involving a behavioral or mental disorder; requiring a licensed certified
7 social worker–clinical to meet certain requirements to perform the evaluation
8 services; and generally relating to the evaluation of permanent impairments by
9 licensed certified social workers–clinical under the workers’ compensation law.
