Assigned to: Environment and Transportation

A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Carbon Removal Technology and Practices – Regulations and Use of Funding

3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of the Environment to adopt regulations
4 governing the permitting of certain carbon removal technology and practices;
5 requiring each State agency to use available funding sources to fund carbon removal
6 projects that utilize certain carbon removal technology and practices; and generally
7 relating to carbon removal technology and practices.

8 BY adding to
9 Article – Environment
10 Section 2–1213
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2013 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

13 Preamble

14 WHEREAS, The current practice of applying solid waste regulations to the process
15 of carbon removal is unsuitable; now, therefore,

16 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
17 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

18 Article – Environment

19 2–1213.

20 (A) ON OR BEFORE JANUARY 1, 2028, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ADOPT
21 REGULATIONS GOVERNING THE PERMITTING OF CARBON REMOVAL TECHNOLOGY
22 AND PRACTICES THAT ARE CERTIFIED BY AN INTERNATIONALLY RECOGNIZED
23 THIRD–PARTY CERTIFICATION BODY.
