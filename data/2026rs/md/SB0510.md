A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 State Board of Morticians and Funeral Directors – Funeral Direction Licensure
3 and Apprentices – Requirements

4 FOR the purpose of altering the education requirements an individual must meet to
5 practice funeral direction or as a licensed apprentice; prohibiting the practical
6 examination qualification required for a license to practice funeral direction from
7 including a practical examination demonstrating competency in the preparation of
8 dead human bodies for a certain purpose; and generally relating to the State Board
9 of Morticians and Funeral Directors.
