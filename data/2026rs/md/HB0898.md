A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Economic Development – Delivering Economic Competitiveness and Advancing
3 Development Efforts (DECADE) Act

4 FOR the purpose of altering the designation, administration, and purposes of and eligibility
5 for certain economic development programs; redesignating the Economic
6 Development Opportunities Program Account to be the Strategic Closing Fund
7 within the Department of Commerce; altering the purposes for and methods by
8 which the Strategic Closing Fund may be utilized; altering the distribution of certain
9 video lottery terminal proceeds; altering the termination date of the Build Our
10 Future Grant Pilot Program, Job Creation Tax Credit, Research and Development
11 Tax Credit, and Employer Security Clearance Costs Tax Credit; altering eligibility
12 for and the calculation of certain tax credits; allowing a qualified investor that is a
13 pass–through entity that pays a certain income tax on behalf of its members to
14 receive and allocate in any manner a credit or refund of a credit under the
15 Biotechnology Investment Incentive Tax Credit; authorizing a qualified film
16 production entity to amend its application for the film production activity tax credit
17 under certain circumstances; repealing a certain limit on the aggregate amount of
18 tax credit certificates that the Secretary may issue for a single film production
19 activity; and generally relating to economic development and economic development
20 initiatives.
