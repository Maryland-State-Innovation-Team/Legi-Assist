A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Baltimore County Code of Public Local Laws – 2025 Edition – Legalization

3 FOR the purpose of legalizing the 2025 Edition of the Baltimore County Code of Public
4 Local Laws and any supplement to the extent to which that code or supplement
5 contains laws enacted by the General Assembly; and generally relating to the 2025
6 Edition of the Baltimore County Code of Public Local Laws.

7 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
8 That the 2025 Edition of the Baltimore County Code of Public Local Laws and any
9 supplement to the 2025 Edition of the Baltimore County Code of Public Local Laws, to the
10 extent to which that code or supplement contains laws enacted by the General Assembly of
11 Maryland, is legalized. The 2025 Edition of the Baltimore County Code of Public Local Laws
12 and any supplement shall be deemed and taken in all the courts of the State and by all
13 public officials of the State and its political subdivisions to be evidence of the law enacted
14 by the General Assembly contained in the code or a supplement to it.

15 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
16 1, 2026.
