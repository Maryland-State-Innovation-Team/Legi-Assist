A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Fiduciary Institutions – Exploitation of Seniors and Vulnerable Adults –
3 Protections and Required Referral
4 (Vulnerable Adult Banking Protection Act)

5 FOR the purpose of authorizing a fiduciary institution, under certain circumstances, to
6 delay or deny a disbursement from the account of a certain individual or the account
7 on which a certain individual is a beneficiary; requiring a fiduciary institution, under
8 certain circumstances, to provide certain financial records to certain entities;
9 authorizing a fiduciary institution, under certain circumstances, to contact certain
10 individuals; providing certain fiduciary institutions immunity from certain liability;
11 and generally relating to disbursement delays, disbursement denials, and
12 disclosures of confidential financial records by fiduciary institutions.

3 (B) (1) A FIDUCIARY INSTITUTION MAY DELAY OR DENY A

9 (II) WITHIN 4 BUSINESS DAYS AFTER THE DATE ON WHICH THE
10 DISBURSEMENT IS REQUESTED, THE FIDUCIARY INSTITUTION:

11 1. SENDS WRITTEN NOTICE OF THE REASON FOR THE
12 DELAY OR THE DENIAL TO ALL PARTIES AUTHORIZED TO TRANSACT BUSINESS ON
13 THE ACCOUNT; AND

14 2. NOTIFIES THE ADULT PROTECTIVE SERVICES
15 PROGRAM IN A LOCAL DEPARTMENT OF SOCIAL SERVICES, A LAW ENFORCEMENT
16 AGENCY, OR A STATE’S ATTORNEY.

17 (2) A FIDUCIARY INSTITUTION:

18 (I) MAY NOT SEND THE WRITTEN NOTICE REQUIRED UNDER
19 PARAGRAPH (1) OF THIS SUBSECTION TO A PARTY THE FIDUCIARY INSTITUTION

21 ENGAGE IN THE FINANCIAL EXPLOITATION OF THE ELIGIBLE ADULT; AND

22 (II) ON REQUEST, SHALL PROVIDE A STATUS UPDATE ON ANY

1 (E) (1) IN ADDITION TO THE NOTICE REQUIRED UNDER SUBSECTION
2 (B)(1) OF THIS SECTION, A FIDUCIARY INSTITUTION THAT REASONABLY BELIEVES
