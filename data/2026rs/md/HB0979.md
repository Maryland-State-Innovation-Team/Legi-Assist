A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 State Constitutional Convention – Question on Ballot – Passage by Majority of
3 Votes Cast

4 FOR the purpose of requiring the General Assembly to organize and hold a Convention to
5 amend the State Constitution if a majority of the votes cast on the question of calling
6 for a Convention, rather than a majority of voters voting at the election, are for the
7 holding of a Convention; and prohibiting any changes or amendments to the State
8 Constitution adopted by the Convention and submitted to the voters of the State
9 from becoming effective unless the majority of votes cast on the question are in favor
10 of the adoption, rather than the changes or amendments being adopted by a majority
11 of the voters voting thereon.
