A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Agriculture – Noxious Weeds – Palmer Amaranth

3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of Agriculture to issue a certain order to a
4 certain owner of land to eradicate Palmer amaranth under certain circumstances;
5 requiring the Department to inspect certain land at a certain frequency under
6 certain circumstances; and generally relating to noxious weeds and Palmer
7 amaranth.

8 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
9 Article – Agriculture
10 Section 9–404 and 9–406
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2016 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

13 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
14 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

15 Article – Agriculture

16 9–404.

17 (a) No person may:

18 (1) Import or transport a noxious weed in the State in any form capable of
19 growth; or

20 (2) Contaminate any uninfested land with a noxious weed through the
21 movement of rootstocks, seed, soil, mulch, nursery stock, farm machinery, or any other
22 artificial medium.

1 (b) [Each] SUBJECT TO SUBSECTION (C) OF THIS SECTION, EACH landowner,
2 including a landowner of public land, or person who possesses and manages land infested
3 with a noxious weed shall eradicate or control the noxious weed on that land by using
4 practices that the Secretary prescribes, including mowing, cultivating, or treating with an
5 approved herbicide.

6 (C) (1) IN THIS SUBSECTION, “PALMER AMARANTH” MEANS THE PLANT
7 SPECIES PALMER AMARANTH (AMARANTHUS PALMERI) THAT IS LISTED AS A
8 NOXIOUS WEED IN REGULATIONS ADOPTED BY THE DEPARTMENT.

9 (2) (I) IF, BASED ON AN INSPECTION BY THE DEPARTMENT, THE
10 DEPARTMENT FINDS PALMER AMARANTH ON LAND, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL
11 ISSUE AN ORDER TO ERADICATE THE PALMER AMARANTH TO THE PERSON THAT
12 OWNS THE LAND.

13 (II) AN ORDER ISSUED UNDER SUBPARAGRAPH (I) OF THIS
14 PARAGRAPH SHALL REQUIRE THE PALMER AMARANTH TO BE ERADICATED WITHIN
15 2 WEEKS AFTER THE ORDER WAS ISSUED.

16 (III) IF THE DEPARTMENT ISSUES AN ORDER UNDER
17 SUBPARAGRAPH (I) OF THIS PARAGRAPH, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL INSPECT THE
18 LAND THAT IS SUBJECT TO THE ORDER EVERY 2 WEEKS UNTIL THE DEPARTMENT
19 DETERMINES THAT THE PALMER AMARANTH HAS BEEN ERADICATED.
