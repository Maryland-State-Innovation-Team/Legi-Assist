2 Frederick County – Sheriff – Salary

3 FOR the purpose of altering the annual salary of the Sheriff of Frederick County to be a
4 certain amount for a certain calendar year; altering the annual salary of the Sheriff

6 for Frederick County for certain subsequent calendar years; and generally relating

1 (I) $125,000 FOR CALENDAR YEAR 2026;

2 (II) $175,000 FOR CALENDAR YEAR 2027; AND

3 (III) 82.5% OF THE STATE’S ATTORNEY FOR FREDERICK
4 COUNTY’S ANNUAL SALARY INCLUDING ANY INCREMENTS AND SALARY
5 ADJUSTMENTS AUTHORIZED UNDER § 15–411(B)(2) OF THE CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
6 ARTICLE FOR CALENDAR YEAR 2028 AND EACH SUBSEQUENT CALENDAR YEAR.

7 (2) ANY CHANGE IN THE SALARY PAID TO THE STATE’S ATTORNEY
8 FOR FREDERICK COUNTY DURING THE TERM OF OFFICE OF THE SHERIFF MAY NOT
9 APPLY DURING THE SHERIFF’S CURRENT TERM OF OFFICE, BUT THE CHANGED RATE
10 SHALL TAKE EFFECT AT THE BEGINNING OF THE NEXT FOLLOWING TERM OF OFFICE.

11 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, in accordance with Article
12 III, § 35 of the Maryland Constitution, this Act may not be construed to extend or apply to
13 the salary or compensation of the Sheriff of Frederick County while serving in a term of
14 office beginning before the effective date of this Act, but the provisions of this Act
15 concerning the salary or compensation of the Sheriff of Frederick County shall take effect
16 at the beginning of the next following term of office. This limitation does not apply to an
17 individual appointed or elected after the effective date of this Act to fill out an unexpired
18 term. A Sheriff of Frederick County whose term begins after the effective date of this Act
19 may not receive during the term of office of the Sheriff an increase in salary or
20 compensation as a result of the increase in the salary of the State’s Attorney for Frederick
21 County if the increase of the salary of the State’s Attorney for Frederick County was not
22 fixed at the time the term of office of the Sheriff began.

23 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
24 October 1, 2026.
