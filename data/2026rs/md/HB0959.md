A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Natural Resources – Establishment of Gwynns Falls State Park

3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Department of Natural Resources to establish Gwynns
4 Falls State Park as a partnership park between the Department and Baltimore City;
5 altering the authorized uses of a certain part of the Park System Capital
6 Improvements and Acquisition Fund; prohibiting the Department from charging an
7 entrance fee to Gwynns Falls State Park; requiring that Gwynns Falls State Park
8 consist of a certain area; requiring the Department, in consultation with Baltimore
9 City, to hire an independent consultant to complete a certain comprehensive park
10 master plan; requiring the Governor to include certain appropriations in the annual
11 budget bill for a certain fiscal year; and generally relating to the establishment of
12 Gwynns Falls State Park.
