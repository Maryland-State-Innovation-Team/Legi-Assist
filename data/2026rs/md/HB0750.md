5 (ii) “Medical facility” includes an agency, clinic, or office operated

8 (b) (1) This section does not apply to:

9 (i) the chief executive officer of the medical facility;

10 (ii) a designee of the chief executive officer of the medical facility;

11 (iii) an agent of the medical facility; or

12 (iv) a law enforcement officer.

13 (2) This section does not prohibit:

14 (i) speech; or

15 (ii) picketing in connection with a labor dispute as defined in §
16 4–301 of the Labor and Employment Article.

17 (c) A person may not intentionally act, alone or with others, to prevent another
18 from entering or exiting a medical facility by physically:

19 (1) detaining the other; or

20 (2) obstructing, impeding, or hindering the other’s passage.

21 (d) A person who violates this section is guilty of a misdemeanor and on conviction
22 is subject to imprisonment not exceeding 90 days or a fine not exceeding $1,000 or both.
