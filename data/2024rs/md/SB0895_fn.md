Baltimore City - Alcoholic Beverages - Licensing Fees 
 
 
This bill authorizes the Baltimore City Board of License Commissioners to (1) increase 
annual, supplementary, and other alcoholic beverages licensing fees for specified licenses 
in Baltimore City; (2) set per diem and annual licensing fees for specified licenses; and 
(3) increase, from $50 to $78, renewal application fees for all alcoholic beverages licenses. 
The bill takes effect July 1, 2024. 
 
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  None. 
 
Local Effect:  Baltimore City licensing and fee revenues may increase by $823,200 
beginning in FY 2025, and annually thereafter. Baltimore City expenditures are not 
affected. 
 
Small Business Effect:  Potential meaningful impact on small business restaurants, bars, 
and other alcoholic beverages license holders due to increased fees under the bill. 
 
 
Analysis 
 
Bill Summary/Current Law:  The bill increases specified alcoholic beverages licensing 
fees in Baltimore City, as detailed in Exhibit 1. 
 
The bill requires the board to set per diem license fees for specified licenses, which are 
currently established in statute, and to set annual license fees for a Class BWLT beer, wine, 
and liquor (on premises) tasting license, as specified. Currently, the annual fees for a
