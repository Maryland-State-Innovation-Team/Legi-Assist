Allegany County - Alcoholic Beverages - Underage Employees 
 
 
This bill authorizes an alcoholic beverages licensee in Allegany County to employ an 
individual who is 16 or 17 years old, as long as that individual does not sell or serve 
alcoholic beverages. The bill takes effect July 1, 2024. 
 
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  None. 
  
Local Effect:  The bill does not materially affect Allegany County operations or finances.  
    
Small Business Effect:  Minimal. 
  
 
Analysis 
 
Current Law:  An alcoholic beverages licensee in Allegany County may employ an 
individual who is at least 18 years of age, and this individual is allowed to sell or serve 
alcoholic beverages. 
 
 
Additional Information 
 
Recent Prior Introductions:  Similar legislation has not been introduced within the last 
three years. 
 
Designated Cross File:  SB 1176 (Senator McKay) - Rules.
