Baltimore County - Alcoholic Beverages - Individual Storage Permits 
 
 
This bill requires retailers in Baltimore County to have written approval from the  
Baltimore County Board of License Commissioners before applying to the 
Executive Director of the Alcohol, Tobacco, and Cannabis Commission (ATCC) for an 
individual storage permit. The bill takes effect July 1, 2024. 
   
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  None.       
  
Local Effect:  Baltimore County can implement the bill with existing resources. 
Baltimore County revenues are not affected.      
  
Small Business Effect:  Potential minimal.  
  
 
Analysis 
 
Current Law:  An individual storage permit authorizes the holder to establish a warehouse 
to store alcoholic beverages in which title to the alcoholic beverages is vested in the permit 
holder. The permit fee is $50.
